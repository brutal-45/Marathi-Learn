# दिवाळी | Diwali

## Overview | अवलोकन

**Diwali (दिवाळी)**, also known as the "Festival of Lights," is one of the most important festivals in Maharashtra and across India. The name comes from the Sanskrit word "Deepavali" (दीपावली), meaning "row of lights" (दिपांची रांग). This five-day festival celebrates the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.

दिवाळी, ज्याला "दिवे उत्सव" म्हणूनही ओळखले जाते, ती महाराष्ट्र आणि संपूर्ण भारतातील सर्वात महत्त्वाच्या सणांपैकी एक आहे. हा पाच दिवसांचा उत्सव प्रकाशाचा अंधारावर, चांगल्याचा वाईटावर आणि ज्ञानाचा अज्ञानावर विजय साजरा करतो.

## Historical & Mythological Significance | ऐतिहासिक आणि पौराणिक महत्त्व

### Return of Lord Rama | श्री रामाचे आगमन
- Celebrates Lord Rama's return to Ayodhya after 14 years of exile
- People lit diyas (oil lamps) to welcome him
- Symbolizes the triumph of good over evil

### Other Legends | इतर कथा

| Legend | Significance |
|--------|--------------|
| Krishna defeats Narakasura | Victory of good over evil |
| Goddess Lakshmi's birthday | Celebration of prosperity |
| Pandavas' return | Victory of truth |
| King Bali's sacrifice | Humility and devotion |

## The Five Days of Diwali | दिवाळीचे पाच दिवस

### Day 1: Vasu Baras (वासू बारस)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | वासू बारस |
| Significance | Worship of cows and calves |
| Ritual | Gopuja (गोपूजा) - Cow worship |
| Meaning | Gratitude for nourishment |

### Day 2: Dhanteras (धनतेरस)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | धनतेरस |
| Significance | Worship of wealth and prosperity |
| God | Lord Dhanvantari (धन्वंतरी) - God of medicine |
| Ritual | Buying gold, silver, or utensils |
| Belief | Brings prosperity throughout the year |

### Day 3: Narak Chaturdashi (नरक चतुर्थी)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | नरक चतुर्थी / छोटी दिवाळी |
| Significance | Victory over demon Narakasura |
| Ritual | Early morning oil bath (अभ्यंग स्नान) |
| Tradition | Lighting lamps to remove darkness |
| Celebration | Firecrackers and sweets |

#### The Abhyanga Snan Tradition | अभ्यंग स्नान परंपरा

The early morning oil bath before sunrise is considered very auspicious:

| Step | Marathi | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | तेल लावणे | Apply sesame oil on body |
| 2 | उबट तेल | Warm oil application |
| 3 | स्नान करणे | Bathe with utna (herbal paste) |
| 4 | नवीन कपडे | Wear new clothes |

### Day 4: Lakshmi Pujan (लक्ष्मी पूजन)

This is the **main day** of Diwali:

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | लक्ष्मी पूजन |
| Significance | Worship of Goddess Lakshmi |
| Time | During Amavasya (new moon night) |
| Ritual | Lakshmi Puja with 5 diyas |

#### Lakshmi Puja Rituals | लक्ष्मी पूजन विधी

| Step | Ritual | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | घर स्वच्छ करणे | Clean and decorate home |
| 2 | रंगोळी | Draw rangoli at entrance |
| 3 | दिवे लावणे | Light diyas everywhere |
| 4 | पूजा सामग्री | Arrange puja items |
| 5 | लक्ष्मी पूजन | Worship Goddess Lakshmi |
| 6 | गणेश पूजन | Worship Lord Ganesha |
| 7 | कुबेर पूजन | Worship Lord of wealth |
| 8 | आरती | Sing aarti |
| 9 | प्रसाद | Distribute prasad |

### Day 5: Bali Pratipada / Padwa (बळी प्रतिपदा / पडवा)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | बळी प्रतिपदा / पडवा |
| Significance | New Year as per Hindu calendar |
| Tradition | Husband-wife celebration |
| Ritual | Gift giving |
| Special | Bali Puja |

### Day 6: Bhai Dooj (भाऊ बीज)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | भाऊ बीज |
| Significance | Brother-sister bond |
| Ritual | Sister applies tilak to brother |
| Tradition | Special meal for brother |
| Meaning | Sister's prayer for brother's long life |

Rangoli is an essential part of Diwali decoration:

### Types of Rangoli | रंगोळीचे प्रकार

| Type | Marathi | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|

### Common Rangoli Designs | सामान्य रंगोळी डिझाइन

## Safety Tips | सुरक्षा टिप्स

### Firecracker Safety | पटाखे सुरक्षा

| Tip | Marathi |
|-----|---------|
| Keep water nearby | पाणी जवळ ठेवा |
| Wear cotton clothes | कॉटन कपडे घाला |
| Light crackers in open space | खुल्या जागी पटाखे फोडा |
| Supervise children | मुलांवर लक्ष ठेवा |
| Keep first aid ready | प्राथमिक उपचार तयार ठेवा |

### Eco-Friendly Diwali | पर्यावरणपूरक दिवाळी

| Practice | Benefit |
|----------|---------|
| Use diyas instead of electric lights | Traditional and eco-friendly |
| Avoid loud crackers | Reduces noise pollution |
| Use natural colors for rangoli | Safe for environment |
| Donate to needy instead of spending on crackers | Social responsibility |
