# इतर सण | Other Festivals

## Overview | अवलोकन

Maharashtra celebrates numerous festivals throughout the year, each with unique traditions and significance. This section covers two important festivals: Makar Sankranti and Navratri.

महाराष्ट्रात वर्षभर अनेक सण साजरे केले जातात, प्रत्येकाच्या अनन्य परंपरा आणि महत्त्वासह. या विभागात दोन महत्त्वाचे सण: मकर संक्रांत आणि नवरात्री समाविष्ट आहेत.

---

## मकर संक्रांत | Makar Sankranti

### Introduction | परिचय

**Makar Sankranti (मकर संक्रांत)** is a harvest festival marking the transition of the Sun into Capricorn (Makara). It is celebrated on January 14th every year and marks the beginning of longer days.

मकर संक्रांत हा एक कापणी सण आहे जो सूर्याचे मकर राशीत प्रवेश दर्शवतो. हा प्रत्येक वर्षी १४ जानेवारीला साजरा केला जातो आणि दीर्घ दिवसांची सुरुवाट दर्शवतो.

### Significance | महत्त्व

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Solar Festival | Based on solar calendar |
| Harvest Time | New crop celebration |
| Uttarayan | Sun's northward journey begins |
| Auspicious Period | Sacred time for rituals |

### Traditional Foods | पारंपारिक पदार्थ

#### Tilgul (तिळगूळ)

|------------|---------|-------------|
| Sesame seeds | तीळ | Til |

| Coconut | नारळ | Coconut (optional) |
| Ghee | तूप | Clarified butter |

| Sweet | Marathi | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Tilgul Ladoo | तिळगूळ लाडू | Sesame-jaggery balls |
| Til Vadi | तिळवडी | Sesame brittle |
| Gul Poli | गूळ पोळी | Jaggery flatbread |
| Bhogi Bhaji | भोगी भाजी | Mixed vegetable dish |

### The Tilgul Tradition | तिळगूळ परंपरा

A unique custom of exchanging tilgul with the greeting:

| Marathi Greeting | Meaning |
|------------------|---------|
| "तिळगूळ घ्या, गोड गोड बोला" | "Accept tilgul and speak sweet words" |
| "तिळगूळ घ्या, आणि गोड बोला, गोड वागा, गोड राहा" | "Take tilgul, speak, behave and live sweetly" |

This tradition symbolizes:
- **Forgiveness** - Forgetting past grievances
- **Friendship** - Strengthening bonds
- **Sweetness** - Spreading positivity

## नवरात्री | Navratri

### Introduction | परिचय

**Navratri (नवरात्री)** meaning "nine nights" is a festival dedicated to the worship of Goddess Durga and her nine forms. It is celebrated with great devotion across Maharashtra.

नवरात्री म्हणजे "नऊ रात्री" हा देवी दुर्गा आणि तिच्या नऊ स्वरूपांच्या पूजेसाठी समर्पित सण आहे. हा महाराष्ट्रभरात खूप भक्तीने साजरा केला जातो.

### The Nine Forms of Goddess | देवीची नऊ स्वरूपे

| Day | Form | Marathi | Significance |
|-----|------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | Shailaputri | शैलपुत्री | Daughter of mountains |
| 2 | Brahmacharini | ब्रह्मचारिणी | The ascetic |
| 3 | Chandraghanta | चंद्रघंटा | Moon-bell adorned |
| 4 | Kushmanda | कूष्मांडा | Creator of universe |
| 5 | Skandamata | स्कंदमाता | Mother of Skanda |
| 6 | Katyayani | कात्यायनी | Warrior goddess |
| 7 | Kalaratri | कालरात्रि | The fierce form |
| 8 | Mahagauri | महागौरी | The radiant one |
| 9 | Siddhidatri | सिद्धिदात्री | Giver of powers |

### Daily Rituals | दैनंदिन विधी

| Day | Ritual | Marathi |
|-----|--------|---------|
| 1-3 | Durga Puja | दुर्गा पूजा |
| 4-6 | Lakshmi Puja | लक्ष्मी पूजा |
| 7-9 | Saraswati Puja | सरस्वती पूजा |

### Navratri Traditions in Maharashtra | महाराष्ट्रातील नवरात्री परंपरा

#### Ghatasthapana (घटस्थापना)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| First Day | Establishing the sacred pot |
| Pot | Filled with water and soil |
| Seeds | Sowing wheat or barley seeds |
| Location | Puja room or temple |
| Duration | Maintained for 9 days |

#### Traditional Observances | पारंपारिक पालन

| Tradition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Fasting | Many observe partial or full fast |
| Vegetarian Food | Avoiding non-vegetarian food |
| No Onion-Garlic | Satvik diet |
| Temple Visits | Daily darshan |
| Aarti | Morning and evening aarti |

### Dussehra/Vijayadashami | दसरा/विजयादशमी

The tenth day marking the end of Navratri:

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Significance | Victory of good over evil |
| Ravana Dahan | Burning of Ravana effigies |
| Durga Visarjan | Immersion of Durga idols |
| New Beginnings | Auspicious for new ventures |
| Ayudha Puja | Worship of tools and vehicles |
