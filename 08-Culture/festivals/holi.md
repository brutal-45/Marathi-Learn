# होळी | Holi

## Overview | अवलोकन

**Holi (होळी)**, known as the "Festival of Colors" (रंगांचा सण), is one of the most joyous and vibrant festivals celebrated in Maharashtra and across India. This spring festival marks the arrival of spring, the victory of good over evil, and celebrates the eternal love of Radha-Krishna.

होळी, ज्याला "रंगांचा सण" म्हणूनही ओळखले जाते, ती महाराष्ट्र आणि संपूर्ण भारतात साजरी केली जाणारी सर्वात आनंदी आणि रंगबेंगीची सणांपैकी एक आहे. हा वसंत ऋतूचा सण वसंत ऋतूचे आगमन, चांगल्याचा वाईटावर विजय आणि राधा-कृष्णाचे शाश्वत प्रेम साजरे करतो.

## Historical & Mythological Significance | ऐतिहासिक आणि पौराणिक महत्त्व

### Story of Prahlad | प्रल्हादाची कथा

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Demon King | Hiranyakashipu (हिरण्यकश्यपू) |
| Devotee Son | Prahlad (प्रल्हाद) - devotee of Lord Vishnu |
| Holika | Demon's sister who was immune to fire |
| Miracle | Prahlad survived, Holika burned |
| Significance | Victory of devotion and good over evil |

### Story of Krishna | श्रीकृष्णाची कथा

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Lord Krishna | Blue-colored skin from drinking poison |
| Playful Nature | Applied color to Radha and other Gopis |
| Tradition | Playing with colors began from this |
| Location | Mathura, Vrindavan |
| Celebration | Lathmar Holi in these regions |

## The Two Days of Holi | होळीचे दोन दिवस

### Day 1: Holika Dahan (होळिका दहन)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | होळिका दहन |
| Timing | Night before Holi |
| Ritual | Lighting of bonfire |
| Significance | Burning of evil |
| Activity | Singing and dancing around fire |

#### Holika Dahan Rituals | होळिका दहन विधी

| Step | Ritual | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | लाकडे गोळा करणे | Collect wood and combustible materials |
| 2 | होळी बांधणे | Build the pyre |
| 3 | पूजा करणे | Worship the pyre |
| 4 | अग्नी प्रज्वलन | Light the bonfire |
| 5 | प्रदक्षिणा | Circumambulate the fire |
| 6 | प्रार्थना | Pray for removal of evil |
| 7 | प्रसाद वाटणे | Distribute prasad |

### Day 2: Rang Panchami (रंग पंचमी)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Marathi Name | रंग पंचमी / धुळवड |
| Timing | Day after Holika Dahan |
| Activity | Playing with colors |
| Significance | Celebration of love and unity |
| Spirit | Forgiveness and new beginnings |
