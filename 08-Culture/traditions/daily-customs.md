# दैनंदिन परंपरा | Daily Customs

## Overview | अवलोकन

Maharashtrian daily customs (मराठी दैनंदिन परंपरा) are an integral part of everyday life. These traditions, passed down through generations, reflect the values, beliefs, and cultural heritage of Maharashtra.

मराठी दैनंदिन परंपरा हा दैनंदिन जीवनाचा अविभाज्य भाग आहे. पिढ्यानपिढ्या स्वीकारलेल्या या परंपरा महाराष्ट्राचे मूल्य, विश्वास आणि सांस्कृतिक वारसा दर्शवतात.

## Morning Rituals | सकाळच्या विधी

### Waking Up | उठणे

| Custom | Marathi | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Early rising | सकाळी लवकर उठणे | Before sunrise considered auspicious |
| Seeing palms | दर्शन | Seeing palms upon waking |
| Prayer | प्रार्थना | Morning prayer |
| Cleanliness | स्वच्छता | Bath and cleanliness |

### Morning Prayer (Karaagre Vasate) | सकाळची प्रार्थना

A traditional prayer recited upon waking:

| Line | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| कराग्रे वसते लक्ष्मी | Goddess Lakshmi resides at the fingertips |
| करमध्ये सरस्वती | Goddess Saraswati in the middle of palms |
| करमूले तु गोविंद | Lord Govinda at the base of hands |
| प्रभाते करदर्शनम् | Morning salutation to hands |

### Puja (पूजा)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Time | Early morning |
| Place | Home temple or prayer area |
| Activities | Lighting lamp, offering flowers, aarti |
| Duration | 15-30 minutes |

#### Daily Puja Items | दैनंदिन पूजा साहित्य

| Item | Marathi | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| Lamp | दिवा | Light for deity |
| Incense | अगरबत्ती | Fragrance |
| Flowers | फुले | Offering |
| Kumkum | कुंकू | Vermilion |
| Rice | अक्षता | Sacred rice grains |
| Water | पाणी | For offering |
| Prasad | प्रसाद | Sweet offering |

## Greeting Customs | नमस्कार परंपरा

### Namaskar (नमस्कार)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | I bow to the divine in you |
| Gesture | Folded hands at chest level |
| Usage | Universal greeting |
| Respect | Shows respect to elders |

### Types of Greetings | नमस्काराचे प्रकार

| Person | Greeting | Marathi |
|--------|----------|---------|
| General | Namaskar | नमस्कार |
| Elders | Touch feet | पाय लागणे | 
| Women | Namaskar | नमस्कार |
| Priests | Pranam | प्रणाम |

### Touching Feet (पाय लागणे)

| Occasion | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Meeting elders | Touch their feet for blessings |
| Festivals | Seek blessings from family elders |
| Before travel | Take blessings before leaving |
| After achievement | Show gratitude and seek blessings |

## Mealtime Customs | जेवणाच्या परंपरा

### Before Meal | जेवणापूर्वी

| Custom | Marathi | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Washing hands | हात धुणे | Clean hands before eating |
| Serving food | अन्न परोपकार | Offer food to deity first |
| Sitting | पाय पसरून बसणे | Sit cross-legged on floor |
| Prayer | ब्रह्मार्पणम् | Recite food prayer |

#### Food Prayer (Brahmarpanam) | अन्न प्रार्थना

| Sanskrit | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| ब्रह्मार्पणं ब्रह्म हविः | The act of offering is Brahman |
| ब्रह्माग्नौ ब्रह्मणा हुतम् | The oblation is Brahman |
| ब्रह्मैव तेन गन्तव्यं | By Brahman it is to be attained |
| ब्रह्म कर्म समाधिना | By one who sees Brahman in action |

### During Meal | जेवणाच्या वेळी

| Custom | Marathi | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Serving order | वाढण्याचा क्रम | Specific order of serving |
| Eating with hands | हाताने जेवणे | Traditional way of eating |
| No waste | अन्न वाया न जाणे | Don't waste food |
| Silence | शांतता | Often eat in silence |

#### Traditional Meal Serving Order | पारंपारिक जेवण वाढण्याचा क्रम

| Order | Item | Marathi |
|-------|------|---------|
| 1 | Salt | मीठ |
| 2 | Pickle | लोणचे |
| 3 | Lemon | लिंबू |
| 4 | Salad | कोशिंबीर |
| 5 | Bhaji | भाजी |
| 6 | Dal/Rassa | डाळ/रस्सा |
| 7 | Rice | भात |
| 8 | Roti/Bhakri | रोटी/भाकरी |
| 9 | Sweet | मिठाई |
| 10 | Buttermilk | ताक |

### After Meal | जेवणानंतर

| Custom | Marathi | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Washing hands | हात धुणे | Clean hands after eating |
| Betel leaf | पान खाणे | Paan after meal (traditional) |
| Rest | आराम | Brief rest after meal |
| Gratitude | कृतज्ञता | Thank the cook/provider |
