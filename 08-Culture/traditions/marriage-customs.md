# विवाह संस्कार | Marriage Customs

## Overview | अवलोकन

Marathi weddings (मराठी लग्न) are vibrant celebrations that blend ancient traditions with joyful festivities. A Maharashtrian marriage is not just a union of two individuals but a coming together of two families, marked by numerous rituals performed over several days.

मराठी लग्ने ही जुन्या परंपरा आणि आनंदी उत्सवांचे मिश्रण असलेली उत्सवात्मक साजरीकरण आहे. महाराष्ट्रीय विवाह हा केवळ दोन व्यक्तींचा संघ नाही तर दोन कुटुंबांचे एकत्र येणे आहे, जे अनेक दिवसांत केलेल्या असंख्य विधींद्वारे चिन्हांकित केले जाते.

## Pre-Wedding Rituals | लग्नापूर्वीचे विधी

### 1. Sakhar Puda (साखर पुडा)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Exchange of sugar packet |
| Significance | Official engagement ceremony |
| Exchange | Bride and groom exchange sugar packets |
| Symbolism | Sweetness in relationship |
| Gift Exchange | Families exchange gifts |

### 2. Kelvan (केळवण)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Feasting before wedding |
| Timing | Few days before wedding |
| Activity | Both families host meals |
| Purpose | Bonding between families |
| Menu | Traditional vegetarian feast |

### 3. Halad Chadavane (हळद चढवणे)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Turmeric application ceremony |
| Marathi Name | हळद चढवणे |
| Timing | Day before wedding |
| Ritual | Applying turmeric paste |
| Purpose | Beautification and purification |
| Significance | Auspicious beginning |

### 4. Ganpati Puja (गणपती पूजा)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Prayer to Lord Ganesha |
| Timing | Before wedding ceremonies |
| Purpose | Removing obstacles |
| Ritual | Worship of Lord Ganesha |
| Prayer | For successful wedding |

## Wedding Day Rituals | लग्नाच्या दिवशीचे विधी   

### 1. Muhurta (मुहूर्त)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Auspicious time |
| Selection | Based on astrology |
| Importance | Main ceremony timing |
| Duration | Few hours window |

### 2. Antarpaat (अंतरपाट)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Curtain between bride and groom |
| Ritual | White cloth held between them |
| Purpose | They cannot see each other |
| Mantras | Priest chants wedding verses |
| Symbolism | Sacred union |

### 3. Mangal Ashtaka (मंगल अष्टक)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Eight sacred verses |
| Ritual | Singing wedding hymns |
| Participants | Priest and family members |
| Timing | During antarpaat |
| Content | Blessings and good wishes |

### 4. Jai Mala (जयमाला)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Exchange of garlands |
| Ritual | Bride and groom exchange garlands |
| Symbolism | Acceptance of each other |
| Fun Element | Playful competition |
| Significance | Official union begins |

### 5. Saptapadi (सप्तपदी)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Seven steps together |
| Ritual | Taking seven vows |
| Direction | Around sacred fire |
| Significance | Seven promises of marriage |
| Completion | Marriage becomes binding |

#### The Seven Vows | सात प्रतिज्ञा

| Step | Vow | Marathi Meaning |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| 1 | Food and nourishment | अन्न आणि पोषण |
| 2 | Strength and prosperity | शक्ती आणि समृद्धी |
| 3 | Wealth and family | संपत्ती आणि कुटुंब |
| 4 | Family and children | कुटुंब आणि मुले |
| 5 | Health and happiness | आरोग्य आणि आनंद |
| 6 | Seasons and longevity | ऋतू आणि दीर्घायुष्य |
| 7 | Friendship and companionship | मैत्री आणि सहचार्य |

### 6. Mangalsutra (मंगळसूत्र)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Sacred thread |
| Ritual | Groom ties it around bride's neck |
| Symbolism | Symbol of married woman |
| Design | Black beads with gold pendant |
| Significance | Protection and marital status |

### 7. Kanyadaan (कन्यादान)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Meaning | Giving away the daughter |
| Ritual | Father gives daughter's hand |
| Symbolism | Transfer of responsibility |
| Emotional | Emotional moment for family |
| Significance | Most important ritual |
