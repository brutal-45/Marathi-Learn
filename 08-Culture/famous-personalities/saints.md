# संत परंपरा | Saints of Maharashtra

## Overview | अवलोकन

Maharashtra has a rich tradition of saint-poets (संत कवी) who revolutionized society through their teachings of devotion (भक्ती), equality (समता), and social reform (सामाजिक सुधारणा). These saints composed their works in Marathi, making spirituality accessible to common people. The two most revered saints are **Sant Dnyaneshwar (संत ज्ञानेश्वर)** and **Sant Tukaram (संत तुकाराम)**.

महाराष्ट्रात संत कवींची समृद्ध परंपरा आहे, ज्यांनी भक्ती, समता आणि सामाजिक सुधारणेच्या शिकवणीद्वारे समाजात क्रांती घडवून आणली. या संतांनी आपली रचना मराठीत केली, ज्यामुळे सामान्य लोकांना अध्यात्म सुलभ झाले. सर्वात पूजनीय संतांमध्ये संत ज्ञानेश्वर आणि संत तुकाराम मुख्य आहेत.

### The Prodigy Saint | बालसंत

Dnyaneshwar showed extraordinary spiritual wisdom from childhood:

| Achievement | Age | Significance |
|-------------|-----|--------------|
| Wrote Dnyaneshwari | 15 | Commentary on Bhagavad Gita |

### Major Works | प्रमुख ग्रंथ

#### Dnyaneshwari (ज्ञानेश्वरी)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Type | Commentary on Bhagavad Gita |
| Language | Marathi (मराठी) |
| Verses | 9,000+ Ovi (ओवी) |
| Purpose | Make Gita accessible to common people |
| Impact | Foundation of Marathi literature |

**Sample Verse (ओवी):**

```
ज्ञानदेव म्हणे ऐका साधकांचिये गोष्टी |
जैसे पाहिले तैसे बोलिले सांडूनिया दोषी ||
```

**Translation**: Listen, O seekers, to the words of Dnyanadeva. I speak exactly as I have seen, without any fault.

|--------|---------|

### Abhangas - Divine Poetry | अभंग - दिव्य कविता

Tukaram composed thousands of Abhangas (devotional songs):

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Total Abhangas | 4,500+ |
| Format | Ovi (ओवी) style |
| Language | Simple Marathi |
| Theme | Devotion to Vithoba |

#### Famous Abhangas | प्रसिद्ध अभंग

**1. About Faith:**
```
जेथे जावें तेथे देव देव म्हणूनिया |
घराचिये देवासी न वचे ध्यानिया ||
```

**Translation**: Wherever I go, I call out to God. But my mind cannot focus on the God within my own home.

**2. About Devotion:**
```
विठ्ठल विठ्ठल जपू न कां |
सुखाचा अनुभव द्यावा सांगून कां ||
```

**Translation**: Why not chant Vithala's name? It gives experience of bliss, why tell others?

**3. About Life's Philosophy:**
```
आतां मी साधिलें सर्व सिद्धांत |
पुढारला तुका म्हणे भक्त ||
```

**Translation**: Now I have understood all principles. Tuka says, devotion is supreme.

## Pandharpur Wari | पंढरपूर वारी

### The Great Pilgrimage | महान यात्रा

The Wari is an annual pilgrimage to Pandharpur:

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Destination | Pandharpur (पंढरपूर) |
| Deity | Lord Vithoba (विठोबा) |
| Duration | 21 days |
| Month | Ashadha (आषाढ) - June-July |
| Participants | Millions (वारकरी) |

### Warkari Tradition | वारकरी परंपरा

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Followers | Warkaris (वारकरी) |
| Practice | Pandharpur pilgrimage |
| Lifestyle | Simple, devotional |
| Belief | Vithoba as family deity |

### Palkhi Procession | पालखी मिरवणूक

| Element | Marathi | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Palkhi | पालखी | Palanquin of saint's padukas |
