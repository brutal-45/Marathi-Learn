## 💡 Tips for Using YouTube Effectively

### Learning Strategy
1. **Start with structured lessons**: Use learning channels for fundamentals
2. **Use subtitles**: Enable Marathi subtitles when available
3. **Slow down playback**: Use 0.75x speed for difficult content
4. **Shadow speaking**: Repeat after the speaker
5. **Take notes**: Pause and write down new vocabulary

### Practice Routine
1. **Morning**: Watch one educational video (10-15 min)
2. **Afternoon**: Listen to songs or news (passive learning)
3. **Evening**: Watch stories or entertainment (enjoyment + learning)

### Tools
- **YouTube Premium**: Download videos for offline learning
- **Playlists**: Create your own learning playlists
- **History**: Review watched videos for revision
