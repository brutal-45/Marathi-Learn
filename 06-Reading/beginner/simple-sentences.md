# Simple Sentences (साधी वाक्ये)

Practice reading basic Marathi sentences for everyday situations.

---

## 1. Greetings (शुभेच्छा)

### देवनागरी
नमस्कार! मी राहुल आहे. तुम्ही कसे आहात?

### Transliteration
Namaskār! Mī Rāhul āhe. Tumhī kase āhāt?

### English Translation
Hello! I am Rahul. How are you?

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| नमस्कार | namaskār | hello, greetings |
| मी | mī | I |
| आहे | āhe | am (is/are) |
| तुम्ही | tumhī | you (formal/plural) |
| कसे | kase | how |
| आहात | āhāt | are |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What is the speaker's name?
2. What greeting is used?
3. How would you respond to this question?

## 2. Family Introduction (परिवार परिचय)

### देवनागरी
माझे नाव सीता आहे. माझे वडील डॉक्टर आहेत. माझी आई शिक्षिका आहे.

### Transliteration
Māje nāv Sītā āhe. Māje vadīl dōktar āhet. Mājhī āī shikshikā āhe.

### English Translation
My name is Sita. My father is a doctor. My mother is a teacher.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| माझे | māje | my (neuter) |
| नाव | nāv | name |
| वडील | vadīl | father |
| डॉक्टर | dōktar | doctor |
| आहेत | āhet | are |
| आई | āī | mother |
| शिक्षिका | shikshikā | teacher (female) |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What is the speaker's name?
2. What is the father's profession?
3. What is the mother's profession?

## 3. Daily Activities (दैनंदिन कृती)

### देवनागरी
मी सकाळी उठतो. मी प्रत्येक दिवस व्यायाम करतो. नंतर मी नाश्ता करतो.

### Transliteration
Mī sakālī uṭhatō. Mī pratyek divas vyāyām kartō. Nantar mī nāshtā kartō.

### English Translation
I wake up in the morning. I exercise every day. Then I have breakfast.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| सकाळी | sakālī | in the morning |
| उठतो | uṭhatō | wake up (male speaker) |
| प्रत्येक | pratyek | every |
| दिवस | divas | day |
| व्यायाम | vyāyām | exercise |
| करतो | kartō | do (male speaker) |
| नंतर | nantar | then, after |
| नाश्ता | nāshtā | breakfast |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. When does the speaker wake up?
2. How often does the speaker exercise?
3. What does the speaker do after exercising?

## 4. At Home (घरी)

### देवनागरी
आमचे घर मोठे आहे. घरात चार खोल्या आहेत. आम्ही घरी एकत्र राहतो.

### Transliteration
Āmche ghar mōṭhe āhe. Gharāt chār khol'yā āhet. Āmhī gharī ekatra rāhatō.

### English Translation
Our house is big. There are four rooms in the house. We live together at home.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| आमचे | āmche | our |
| घर | ghar | house |
| मोठे | mōṭhe | big |
| घरात | gharāt | in the house |
| चार | chār | four |
| खोल्या | khol'yā | rooms |
| आम्ही | āmhī | we |
| एकत्र | ekatra | together |
| राहतो | rāhatō | live |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. Is the house small or big?
2. How many rooms are there?
3. Who lives in the house?

## 5. Food (अन्न)

### देवनागरी
मला पोळी आवडते. मी दररोज भात खातो. पाणी पिणे चांगले आहे.

### Transliteration
Malā pōlī āvdate. Mī darroj bhāt khātō. Pānī pīne chāngle āhe.

### English Translation
I like poli (flatbread). I eat rice every day. Drinking water is good.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| मला | malā | to me |
| पोळी | pōlī | flatbread |
| आवडते | āvdate | like/pleases |
| दररोज | darroj | every day |
| भात | bhāt | rice |
| खातो | khātō | eat (male speaker) |
| पाणी | pānī | water |
| पिणे | pīne | to drink |
| चांगले | chāngle | good |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What food does the speaker like?
2. What does the speaker eat daily?
3. What is good for health according to the text?

Ātā sandhyākāl āhe. Sāt vājle āhet. Mī abhyās kartō.

### English Translation
Now it is evening. It is seven o'clock. I am studying.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| आता | ātā | now |
| संध्याकाळ | sandhyākāl | evening |
| सात | sāt | seven |
| वाजले | vājle | o'clock (struck) |
| अभ्यास | abhyās | study |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What time of day is it?
2. What time is it?
3. What is the speaker doing?

## Summary

### Key Sentence Patterns Used

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Introduction | मी [नाव] आहे. (I am [name].) |
| Possession | माझे [object] आहे. (I have [object].) |
| Preference | मला [object] आवडते. (I like [object].) |
| Location | [Location] आहे. (It is [location].) |
| Action | मी [verb] करतो. (I do [action].) |

### Practice Tips
1. Read each sentence aloud 3-5 times
2. Try to form your own sentences using the patterns
3. Write the sentences in Devanagari script
4. Practice with a language partner
