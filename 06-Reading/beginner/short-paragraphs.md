# Short Paragraphs (लघु परिच्छेद)

Practice reading short passages in Marathi. Each passage contains 3-5 sentences.

---

## 1. My Family (माझा परिवार)

### देवनागरी
माझा परिवार लहान आहे. आमच्या घरी पाच जण आहेत. माझे वडील, आई, भाऊ, बहीण आणि मी. माझे वडील शिक्षक आहेत. आई घरी काम करतात. आम्ही एकत्र राहतो आणि एकत्र जेवतो.

### Transliteration
Mājhā parivār lahān āhe. Āmchyā gharī pāch jan āhet. Māje vadīl, āī, bhāū, bahīṇ āṇi mī. Māje vadīl shikshak āhet. Āī gharī kām kartāt. Āmhī ekatra rāhatō āṇi ekatra jevtō.

### English Translation
My family is small. There are five people in our house. My father, mother, brother, sister, and me. My father is a teacher. Mother works at home. We live together and eat together.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| परिवार | parivār | family |
| लहान | lahān | small |
| जण | jan | people (counter) |
| भाऊ | bhāū | brother |
| बहीण | bahīṇ | sister |
| आणि | āṇi | and |
| शिक्षक | shikshak | teacher (male) |
| काम | kām | work |
| जेवतो | jevtō | eat (dine) |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. How many family members are there?
2. What is the father's profession?
3. Where does the mother work?
4. What two things does the family do together?

## 2. My Village (माझे गाव)

### देवनागरी
माझे गाव खूप सुंदर आहे. गावात हिरवे शेत आहे. नदी जवळून वाहते. लोक शेती करतात. गावात एक मोठे मंदिर आहे. लोक मंदिरात प्रार्थना करतात. मला माझे गाव खूप आवडते.

### Transliteration
Māje gāv khūp sundar āhe. Gāvāt hirave shet āhe. Nadī javalūn vāhate. Lok shetī kartāt. Gāvāt ek mōṭhe mandir āhe. Lok mandirāt prārthanā kartāt. Malā māje gāv khūp āvdate.

### English Translation
My village is very beautiful. There are green fields in the village. A river flows nearby. People do farming. There is a big temple in the village. People pray in the temple. I like my village very much.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| गाव | gāv | village |
| खूप | khūp | very |
| सुंदर | sundar | beautiful |
| हिरवे | hirave | green |
| शेत | shet | field |
| नदी | nadī | river |
| वाहते | vāhate | flows |
| लोक | lok | people |
| शेती | shetī | farming |
| मंदिर | mandir | temple |
| प्रार्थना | prārthanā | prayer |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What makes the village beautiful?
2. What flows near the village?
3. What do people do for work?
4. Where do people pray?

## 3. Morning Routine (सकाळची दिनचर्या)

### देवनागरी
मी पहाटे सहा वाजता उठतो. प्रथम मी देवाचे नाव घेतो. मग हात-पाय धुतो. नंतर मी चहा पितो. सकाळी मी व्यायाम करतो. नंतर आई नाश्ता बनवते. आम्ही सगळे एकत्र नाश्ता करतो.

### Transliteration
Mī pahāte sahā vājtā uṭhatō. Pratham mī devāche nāv ghetō. Mag hāt-pāy dhutō. Nantar mī chahā pītō. Sakālī mī vyāyām kartō. Nantar āī nāshtā banavate. Āmhī sagle ekatra nāshtā kartō.

### English Translation
I wake up at six in the morning. First, I take God's name. Then I wash my hands and feet. After that, I drink tea. In the morning, I exercise. Then mother makes breakfast. We all have breakfast together.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| पहाटे | pahāte | early morning |
| सहा | sahā | six |
| वाजता | vājtā | at (time) |
| प्रथम | pratham | first |
| देवाचे नाव घेणे | devāche nāv ghaṇe | to pray (take God's name) |
| मग | mag | then |
| हात-पाय | hāt-pāy | hands and feet |
| धुतो | dhutō | wash |
| चहा | chahā | tea |
| पितो | pītō | drink |
| बनवते | banavate | makes |
| सगळे | sagle | all |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What time does the speaker wake up?
2. What is the first thing the speaker does?
3. What does the speaker drink?
4. Who makes breakfast?

## 4. The Market (बाजार)

### देवनागरी
रविवारी आई बाजारात जातात. बाजारात भीड असते. तेथे भाजी, फळे आणि फुले मिळतात. आई ताजी भाजी विकत घेतात. किंमत योग्य असते. मी बाजारात बरोबर जातो. मला बाजार आवडतो.

### Transliteration
Ravivārī āī bājārāt jātāt. Bājārāt bhīd asate. Tethe bhājī, phale āṇi phule miḷatāt. Āī tājhī bhājī vikat ghetāt. Kimmat yogyā asate. Mī bājārāt barobar jātō. Malā bājār āvdatō.

### English Translation
On Sunday, mother goes to the market. There is a crowd in the market. Vegetables, fruits, and flowers are available there. Mother buys fresh vegetables. The price is reasonable. I go along to the market. I like the market.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| रविवारी | ravivārī | on Sunday |
| बाजार | bājār | market |
| भीड | bhīd | crowd |
| तेथे | tethe | there |
| भाजी | bhājī | vegetables |
| फळे | phale | fruits |
| फुले | phule | flowers |
| मिळतात | miḷatāt | are available |
| ताजी | tājhī | fresh |
| विकत घेणे | vikat gheṇe | to buy |
| किंमत | kimmat | price |
| योग्य | yogyā | reasonable, appropriate |
| बरोबर | barobar | together, along with |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. When does mother go to the market?
2. What three things are available in the market?
3. What does mother buy?
4. Who goes with mother to the market?

## 5. Rainy Day (पावसाळी दिवस)

### देवनागरी
आज पाऊस पडत आहे. आकाशात काळे ढग आहेत. वारा वाहत आहे. पक्षी घरट्यात आहेत. मुले बाहेर खेळत नाहीत. आम्ही घरी आहोत. मी पुस्तक वाचत आहे. पावसाळी हंगाम मला आवडतो.

### Transliteration
Āj pāūs paḍat āhe. Ākāshāt kāle dhang āhet. Vārā vāhat āhe. Pakshī gharṭyāt āhet. Mule bāher kheḷat nāhīt. Āmhī gharī āhot. Mī pustak vāchat āhe. Pāvsālī hangām malā āvdatō.

### English Translation
Today it is raining. There are black clouds in the sky. The wind is blowing. Birds are in their nests. Children are not playing outside. We are at home. I am reading a book. I like the rainy season.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| पाऊस | pāūs | rain |
| पडत | paḍat | falling |
| काळे | kāle | black |
| ढग | dhang | clouds |
| वारा | vārā | wind |
| वाहत | vāhat | blowing |
| पक्षी | pakshī | birds |
| घरटे | gharṭe | nest |
| मुले | mule | children |
| बाहेर | bāher | outside |
| खेळणे | kheḷaṇe | to play |
| नाही | nāhī | no/not |
| पुस्तक | pustak | book |
| वाचत | vāchat | reading |
| हंगाम | hangām | season |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What is the weather like today?
2. What is in the sky?
3. Where are the birds?
4. What is the speaker doing at home?

## 6. Festival (सण)

### देवनागरी
दिवाळी मराठी लोकांचा मोठा सण आहे. लोक नवीन कपडे घालतात. घरी दिवे लावतात. पाटीभोवती रांगोळी काढतात. मिठाई बनवतात आणि खातात. लोक एकमेकांना भेट देतात. मुलांना फटाके आवडतात. हा सण आनंदाचा असतो.

### Transliteration
Divālī marāṭhī lokānchā mōṭhā saṇ āhe. Lok navīn kapade ghālatāt. Gharī dive lāvatāt. Pāṭībhōvatī rāngolī kāḍhatāt. Mithāī banavatāt āṇi khātāt. Lok ekamekānnā bhet detāt. Mulānnā phaṭake āvdatāt. Hā saṇ ānandāchā asatō.

### English Translation
Diwali is a big festival of Marathi people. People wear new clothes. They light lamps at home. They draw rangoli around the courtyard. They make and eat sweets. People visit each other. Children like firecrackers. This festival is full of joy.

### Vocabulary List (शब्दसंग्रह)

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| दिवाळी | divālī | Diwali |
| सण | saṇ | festival |
| नवीन | navīn | new |
| कपडे | kapade | clothes |
| घालणे | ghālaṇe | to wear |
| दिवे | dive | lamps |
| लावणे | lāvaṇe | to light |
| पाटी | pāṭī | courtyard |
| रांगोळी | rāngolī | rangoli (floor art) |
| काढणे | kāḍhaṇe | to draw |
| मिठाई | mithāī | sweets |
| भेट देणे | bhet deṇe | to visit |
| फटाके | phaṭake | firecrackers |
| आनंद | ānand | joy, happiness |

### Comprehension Questions (प्रश्न)
1. What festival is being described?
2. What do people wear during the festival?
3. What do people draw around the courtyard?
4. What do children like during the festival?

### Reading Tips
1. Read the whole paragraph first without stopping
2. Identify words you know
3. Use context to guess unknown words
4. Read again more slowly for understanding
5. Answer questions to check comprehension
