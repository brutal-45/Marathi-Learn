# Philosophical Writings (तात्त्विक लेखन)

Sacred and philosophical texts from Marathi saint literature including Dnyaneshwari, Tukaram Gatha, and other saints' writings.

## 1. ज्ञानेश्वरी - संत ज्ञानेश्वर (Dnyaneshwari - Sant Dnyaneshwar)

### Author Introduction (संत परिचय)

**संत ज्ञानेश्वर (१२७५-१२९६)**

संत ज्ञानेश्वर, ज्यांना ज्ञानदेव म्हणूनही ओळखले जाते, हे मराठीतील सर्वात महत्त्वाचे संत कवी आहेत. त्यांनी "ज्ञानेश्वरी" (भावार्थदीपिका) या ग्रंथात भगवद्गीतेचा मराठीत ओवीबद्ध अनुवाद केला. त्यांच्या लेखनात अद्वैत तत्त्वज्ञानाचे सोपे आणि सुंदर प्रतिपादन आहे. त्यांनी अवघ्या वीस वर्षांच्या आयुष्यात अमर काव्य निर्माण केले.

**Sant Dnyaneshwar (1275-1296)**

Sant Dnyaneshwar, also known as Jnanadev, is the most important saint poet in Marathi. In "Dnyaneshwari" (Bhavarthadipika), he translated the Bhagavad Gita into Marathi in the ovi verse form. His writing presents Advaita philosophy simply and beautifully. In just twenty years of life, he created immortal poetry.

### Excerpt: अध्याय २, ओवी २५० (Chapter 2, Ovi 250)

#### देवनागरी
कर्म करिता कर्मफळ नुपजे ।
निष्कर्मच तो अवश्य जो होये ।
तैसा जो निष्कर्म होय सकळ ।
तयाचेच पाव हे निर्मळ ॥

जसे कां दीपक जळो निजतेजे ।
तेजाच होऊन तेज विभाजे ।
तैसे ज्ञानदेव म्हणे आम्ही ।
तुझेच रूप असे नारायणी ॥

#### Transliteration
Karma karitā karmaphaḷa nupajē |
Niṣkarmaca tō avaśya jō hōyē |
Taisā jō niṣkarma hōya sakaḷa |
Tayācēca pāva hē nirmāḷa ||

Jasē kāṃ dīpaka jaḷō nijatējē |
Tējāca hōūna tēja vibhājē |
Taisē jñānadēva mhaṇē āmhī |
Tujhēca rūpa asē nārāyaṇī ||

#### English Translation
While performing action, the fruit of action does not arise.
One truly becomes actionless (free from bondage of action).
One who thus becomes actionless in all respects,
His state is indeed pure.

Just as a lamp burns by its own light,
Becoming light itself, it distributes light.
Similarly, says Jnanadev, we are
Your very form, O Narayani.

### Philosophical Analysis (तात्त्विक विश्लेषण)

**Core Teaching:** This verse explains the concept of Nishkama Karma - performing actions without attachment to results. When one acts without desire for fruits, one becomes truly free.

**Metaphor Used:** The lamp metaphor beautifully illustrates how the self can be both the source and manifestation of divine light. Just as a lamp becomes one with its light, the individual soul merges with the divine.

**Key Vocabulary:**

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| कर्म | karma | action |
| कर्मफळ | karmaphaḷa | fruit of action |
| निष्कर्म | niṣkarma | free from action-bondage |
| निर्मळ | nirmāḷa | pure |
| दीपक | dīpaka | lamp |
| तेज | tēja | light, brilliance |
| नारायणी | nārāyaṇī | divine feminine |

## 2. तुकाराम गाथा - संत तुकाराम (Tukaram Gatha - Sant Tukaram)

### Author Introduction (संत परिचय)

**संत तुकाराम (१६०८-१६४९)**

संत तुकाराम हे महाराष्ट्रातील सर्वात प्रिय संत कवी आहेत. त्यांनी "अभंग" या काव्यरूपात विठ्ठलभक्तीचे अमूल्य अभिव्यक्ती केली आहे. त्यांच्या गाथेत सुमारे ४५०० अभंग आहेत. त्यांची भाषा सोपी, पण अर्थ गहन आहे. त्यांनी समाजातील अनिष्ट प्रथांवर टीका केली आणि भक्तीमार्ग सर्वांसाठी खुला केला.

**Sant Tukaram (1608-1649)**

Sant Tukaram is the most beloved saint poet of Maharashtra. In "Abhanga" verse form, he expressed priceless devotion to Vitthal. His Gatha contains about 4500 abhangas. His language is simple but the meaning is profound. He criticized harmful social practices and opened the path of devotion for everyone.

### Excerpt: अभंग - "विठ्ठल विठ्ठल जपू"

#### देवनागरी
विठ्ठल विठ्ठल जपू नामाचे ।
विठ्ठल विठ्ठल म्हणतां उमटे चरणाचे ॥ध्रु.॥

नाम घ्यावें रघुनायकाचें ।
मन रांगे चरणीं श्रीहरीच्या ॥१॥

नाम घेतां उमटती चरण ।
तेणें सुखावला चित्त अर्जुन ॥२॥

तुका म्हणे काय सांगावें ।
विठ्ठल विठ्ठल म्हणतां विठ्ठलचि होवें ॥३॥

#### Transliteration
Viṭṭhala viṭṭhala japū nāmāche |
Viṭṭhala viṭṭhala mhaṇatāṃ umaṭē caraṇāche ||Dhru.||

Nāma ghyāvēṃ raghunāyakāceṃ |
Mana rāṅgē caraṇīṃ śrīharicyā ||1||

Nāma ghētāṃ umaṭatī caraṇa |
Tēṇēṃ sukhāvalā citta arjuna ||2||

Tukā mhaṇē kāya sāṅgāvēṃ |
Viṭṭhala viṭṭhala mhaṇatāṃ viṭṭhalaci hōvēṃ ||3||

#### English Translation
Chant Vitthal, Vitthal, the name.
Saying Vitthal, Vitthal, feet appear. ||Refrain||

Take the name of the Lord,
The mind wanders at the feet of Lord Hari. ||1||

Taking the name, feet appear,
Thereby Arjun's mind was delighted. ||2||

Tuka says, what to say,
Saying Vitthal, Vitthal, one becomes Vitthal. ||3||

### Philosophical Analysis (तात्त्विक विश्लेषण)

**Core Teaching:** The abhanga emphasizes the transformative power of chanting the divine name (Nama Smarana). Through constant repetition of "Vitthal Vitthal," the devotee not only experiences the divine presence but ultimately becomes one with it.

**Path of Devotion:** Tukaram teaches that simple nama-japa (chanting) is accessible to all and leads to the highest spiritual realization. The name and the named become identical through sincere devotion.

**Key Vocabulary:**

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| जपणे | japaṇe | to chant |
| नाम | nāma | name |
| चरण | caraṇa | feet |
| उमटणे | umaṭaṇe | to appear, manifest |
| ध्रुवपद | dhruvapada | refrain |
| चित्त | citta | mind, consciousness |
| सुखावणे | sukhāvaṇe | to be delighted |

## 3. संत एकनाथ - एकनाथी भागवत (Sant Eknath - Eknathi Bhagavat)

### Author Introduction (संत परिचय)

**संत एकनाथ (१५३३-१५९९)**

संत एकनाथ हे वारकरी संप्रदायातील महत्त्वाचे संत होते. त्यांनी "एकनाथी भागवत" आणि "भावार्थ रामायण" या ग्रंथांची रचना केली. त्यांनी समाजातील भेदभाव दूर करण्यासाठी अनेक कार्य केली. त्यांच्या भजनात सगळ्या जाती-धर्माच्या लोकांना सहभागी केले.

**Sant Eknath (1533-1599)**

Sant Eknath was an important saint in the Warkari tradition. He composed "Eknathi Bhagavat" and "Bhavarth Ramayan." He did much work to remove discrimination in society. He included people of all castes and religions in his devotional singing.

### Excerpt: अभंग - जातिभेद

#### देवनागरी
जातिभेद नाही भक्तीत ।
सांडिला अहंकार सर्व जन्मभरची ॥

आम्ही सर्व एका परमेश्वराचे ।
कोण उच्च कोण नीच भक्तीत ॥

पाणी भरून वेगवेगळ्या घडग्यात ।
तरीही पाणी तेच कोठेही जावे ॥

तुका म्हणे ऐसेच जीवभाव ।
नाना रूपी एकच परमेश्वर ॥

#### Transliteration
Jātibhēda nāhī bhaktīta |
Sāṇḍilā ahaṃkāra sarva janmabharacī ||

Āmhī sarva ekā parameśvarāche |
Kōṇa ucca kōṇa nīca bhaktīta ||

Pāṇī bharūna vēgavēgaḷyā ghaḍagyāta |
Tarīhī pāṇī tēca kōṭhēhī jāvē ||

Tukā mhaṇē aisēca jīvabhāva |
Nānā rūpī ekaca parameśvara ||

#### English Translation
There is no caste distinction in devotion.
Ego has been abandoned for entire lifetime. ||

We all belong to one God.
Who is high, who is low in devotion? ||

Water filled in different vessels,
Still the water remains the same wherever it goes. ||

Tuka says, such is the nature of soul.
In many forms, there is only one God. ||

### Philosophical Analysis (तात्त्विक विश्लेषण)

**Core Teaching:** Eknath's abhanga delivers a powerful message against caste discrimination. He uses the metaphor of water in different vessels to explain that the divine essence is the same in all beings, regardless of external differences.

**Social Reform:** This verse reflects Eknath's revolutionary approach to social equality within the framework of bhakti. He established that in the realm of devotion, all social hierarchies are meaningless.

**Key Vocabulary:**

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| जातिभेद | jātibhēda | caste distinction |
| भक्ती | bhaktī | devotion |
| अहंकार | ahaṃkāra | ego, pride |
| उच्च | ucca | high |
| नीच | nīca | low |
| घडगे | ghaḍagē | vessels, pots |
| जीवभाव | jīvabhāva | nature of soul |

## 4. संत नामदेव - आद्य वारकरी संत (Sant Namdev - First Warkari Saint)

### Author Introduction (संत परिचय)

**संत नामदेव (१२७०-१३५०)**

संत नामदेव हे वारकरी संप्रदायाचे आद्य संत मानले जातात. ते शिंपी (शिंपणीकर्मी) होते. त्यांच्या अभंगांत बालभक्तीचे आणि निष्ठेचे सुंदर दर्शन होते. विठ्ठलावर त्यांचे अपार प्रेम होते. त्यांचे काही अभंग पंजाबीतही आढळतात.

**Sant Namdev (1270-1350)**

Sant Namdev is considered the first saint of the Warkari tradition. He was a tailor by profession. His abhangas beautifully display childlike devotion and faith. He had immense love for Vitthal. Some of his verses are also found in Punjabi.

### Excerpt: अभंग - "देवा तुझिया गाठी"

#### देवनागरी
देवा तुझिया गाठी न मज काही ।
तुझे चरण आठवले सोडूनिया आठी ॥ध्रु.॥

मी तुझा व्हावें तुझीये कृपे ।
तुझे चरणीं राहावें हेच मज पाठी ॥१॥

जैसा पिता तैसा पुत्र मानो ।
पुत्रासी काय आठवे पित्याची गाठी ॥२॥

नामा म्हणे देवा तुझिया गाठी ।
बाळ इये कैसे सांगे बापाची गाठी ॥३॥

#### Transliteration
Dēvā tujhiyā gāṭhī na maj kāhī |
Tujhe caraṇa āṭhavale sōḍūniyā āṭhī ||Dhru.||

Mī tujhā vhāvēṃ tujhiyē kṛpē |
Tujhe caraṇīṃ rāhāvēṃ hēca maj pāṭhī ||1||

Jaisā pitā taisā putra mānō |
Putrāsī kāya āṭhave pityācī gāṭhī ||2||

Nāmā mhaṇē dēvā tujhiyā gāṭhī |
Bāḷa iye kaisē sāṅgē bāpācī gāṭhī ||3||

#### English Translation
O Lord, I know nothing about You.
Remembering Your feet, I forget all else. ||Refrain||

May I become Yours by Your grace,
To remain at Your feet, this is my only support. ||1||

As is the father, so is the son considered,
What does the son know about the father? ||2||

Nama says, O Lord, about You,
How can a child describe the father? ||3||

### Philosophical Analysis (तात्त्विक विश्लेषण)

**Core Teaching:** Namdev expresses the humility of a devotee who recognizes the vast gap between the divine and the human, yet maintains intimate relationship like that between father and child.

**Devotional Attitude:** The abhanga reflects the intimate, personal relationship with God that characterizes the Warkari tradition - seeing God as a loving parent.

**Key Vocabulary:**

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| गाठ | gāṭha | acquaintance, knowledge |
| कृपा | kṛpā | grace |
| पाठीं | pāṭhīṃ | support, back |
| पिता | pitā | father |
| पुत्र | putra | son |
| बाळ | bāḷa | child |

## 5. समर्थ रामदास - दासबोध (Samarth Ramdas - Dasbodh)

### Author Introduction (संत परिचय)

**समर्थ रामदास (१६०८-१६८१)**

समर्थ रामदास स्वामी हे मराठी संत आणि तत्त्वज्ञ होते. त्यांनी "दासबोध" आणि "मनाचे श्लोक" या ग्रंथांची रचना केली. त्यांनी छत्रपती शिवाजी महाराजांना आध्यात्मिक मार्गदर्शन केले. त्यांच्या लेखनात कर्मयोग, भक्ती आणि ज्ञान यांचा समन्वय आढळतो.

**Samarth Ramdas (1608-1681)**

Samarth Ramdas Swami was a Marathi saint and philosopher. He composed "Dasbodh" and "Manache Shlok." He provided spiritual guidance to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. His writing shows integration of karma yoga, bhakti, and jnana.

### Excerpt: दासबोध, दशक १, समास ४

#### देवनागरी
मनास लागे साधन तेथे मनीच्या वेधा ।
अधिक साधन तेथे पैल पार नेणे कोण वेधा ॥

प्रीती ते नाम एक यापरी सांगावे ।
लागले साधन तेथे दुःखाचे भान हरे ॥

जैसे तेजाचे अंश सूर्य तैसे जनी जनी ।
ऐसे जाणतां जाणणे हेच असे ज्ञान ॥

#### Transliteration
Manāsa lāgē sādhana tēthē manīcyā vēdhā |
Adhika sādhana tēthē paila pāra nēṇē kōṇa vēdhā ||

Prītī tē nāma eka yāparī sāṅgāvē |
Lāgale sādhana tēthē duḥkhāce bhāna harē ||

Jaisē tējāce aṃśa sūrya taisē janī janī |
Aisē jāṇatāṃ jāṇaṇē hēca asē jñāna ||

#### English Translation
Where the mind gets absorbed in practice, there it pierces within.
With more practice, who can know the limits of that piercing?

Love, that is the one name to be told in this way.
When practice begins, the awareness of sorrow disappears.

As the sun is a part of light, so in every birth,
Understanding this way, this alone is knowledge.

### Philosophical Analysis (तात्त्विक विश्लेषण)

**Core Teaching:** Ramdas emphasizes the importance of spiritual practice (sadhana) and love (priti) as the means to transcend sorrow and attain knowledge. The relationship between the individual soul and the divine is compared to the sun and its rays.

**Practical Philosophy:** Unlike purely theoretical philosophy, Dasbodh provides practical guidance for spiritual seekers at all levels.

**Key Vocabulary:**

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| साधन | sādhana | spiritual practice |
| वेध | vēdha | piercing, penetration |
| पार | pāra | limit, boundary |
| प्रीती | prītī | love |
| अंश | aṃśa | part, fragment |
| ज्ञान | jñāna | knowledge |
| दासबोध | dāsabōdha | servant's wisdom |

### Key Philosophical Concepts

**1. नामस्मरण (Nama Smarana)**
- Chanting the divine name
- Simplest path to liberation
- Accessible to all

**2. निष्काम कर्म (Nishkama Karma)**
- Selfless action without desire for results
- Path taught in Bhagavad Gita
- Explained in Dnyaneshwari

**3. सगुण आणि निर्गुण उपासना**
- Saguna: Worship of God with form (like Vitthal)
- Nirguna: Worship of formless divine
- Both paths lead to the same goal

**4. गुरुमार्ग**
- Importance of Guru in spiritual journey
- Guru provides guidance and removes ignorance
- Guru-shishya tradition in Maharashtra

## Cultural Context (सांस्कृतिक संदर्भ)

### The Warkari Tradition (वारकरी संप्रदाय)

The Warkari tradition is a major bhakti movement in Maharashtra. Key aspects include:

**Pilgrimage (वारी):**
- Annual pilgrimage to Pandharpur
- Millions of devotees walk together
- Carrying the padukas of saints

**Core Beliefs:**
- Equality of all before Vitthal
- Simple living and high thinking
- Importance of nama-japa
- Service to all beings

**Major Pilgrimage Sites:**
- पंढरपूर (Pandharpur) - Main shrine of Vitthal
