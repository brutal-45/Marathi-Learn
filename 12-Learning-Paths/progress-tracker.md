# Progress Tracker

Track your Marathi learning journey with these templates. Use them alongside any learning program to monitor your progress and stay motivated.

## 📋 How to Use This Tracker

1. **Copy these templates** to your personal notes or print them
2. **Fill them daily/weekly** as you study
3. **Review monthly** to see your progress
4. **Celebrate milestones** when you reach them

## 💡 Tips for Effective Tracking

1. **Be consistent** - Fill out your tracker at the same time each day
2. **Be honest** - Accurate tracking helps identify areas for improvement
3. **Be specific** - Note what exactly you studied, not just "studied"
4. **Review regularly** - Look back at previous weeks to see progress
5. **Celebrate wins** - Acknowledge achievements, big and small
6. **Adjust as needed** - Modify goals based on what's realistic

---

## 📱 Digital Tracking Options

If you prefer digital tracking, consider:
- **Notion** - Create databases with templates
- **Google Sheets** - Track with formulas and charts
- **Habitica** - Gamify your tracking
- **Anki** - Track vocabulary with spaced repetition
- **Forest** - Track study time with focus sessions

---

**Remember:** Consistent tracking leads to consistent progress! 🎯
