# 30-Day Marathi Learning Challenge

A structured 30-day program to build a solid foundation in Marathi. Perfect for motivated beginners who can dedicate 30-60 minutes daily.

## 📋 Program Overview

| Week | Focus Area | Daily Time |
|------|------------|------------|
| Week 1 | Alphabet & Pronunciation | 45-60 min |
| Week 2 | Basic Grammar & Numbers | 45-60 min |
| Week 3 | Vocabulary & Conversations | 45-60 min |
| Week 4 | Practice & Consolidation | 45-60 min |

---

## Week 1: Alphabet & Pronunciation

### Day 1: Introduction & Vowels (स्वर)
**Goals:**
- Understand the Devanagari script system
- Learn all 12 vowels (अ-अः)
- Practice basic pronunciation

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Read [Alphabet Introduction](../01-Alphabet/README.md)
- [ ] Study [Vowels](../01-Alphabet/vowels.md)
- [ ] Practice writing each vowel 10 times
- [ ] Listen to vowel pronunciations (use resources from [09-Resources](../09-Resources/))

**Resources:** Vowels chart, audio pronunciation guide

**Checkpoint:** Can you recognize and pronounce all 12 vowels?

---

### Day 2: Consonants Part 1 (व्यंजन - First Group)
**Goals:**
- Learn velar and palatal consonants (क ख ग घ ङ, च छ ज झ ञ)
- Understand voiced/unvoiced distinction
- Practice writing

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Study [Consonants](../01-Alphabet/consonants.md) - First 10 consonants
- [ ] Practice writing each consonant 10 times
- [ ] Record yourself pronouncing each consonant

**Resources:** Consonants chart, pronunciation audio

**Checkpoint:** Can you correctly pronounce क vs ख, ग vs घ?

---

### Day 3: Consonants Part 2 (Retroflex & Dental)
**Goals:**
- Learn retroflex and dental consonants (ट ठ ड ढ ण, त थ द ध न)
- Master the crucial difference between dental and retroflex sounds

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Study retroflex and dental consonants
- [ ] Practice distinguishing ट vs त, ड vs द
- [ ] Complete [Practice Sheets](../01-Alphabet/exercises/practice-sheets.md)

**Resources:** Audio comparison files, mirror for mouth position

**Checkpoint:** Can you distinguish त and ट, द and ड?

---

### Day 4: Consonants Part 3 (Labial & Remaining)
**Goals:**
- Learn remaining consonants (प फ ब भ म, य र ल व, श ष स ह, ळ क्ष ज्ञ)
- Complete the alphabet

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Study remaining consonants
- [ ] Practice full alphabet recitation
- [ ] Take [Alphabet Quiz](../10-Practice/quizzes/alphabet-quiz.md)

**Resources:** Full alphabet chart

**Checkpoint:** Can you recite all consonants in order?

---

### Day 5: Barakhadi (बाराखडी)
**Goals:**
- Understand how consonants combine with vowels
- Learn the barakhadi system

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Study [Barakhadi](../01-Alphabet/barakhadi.md)
- [ ] Practice writing barakhadi for क, ख, ग
- [ ] Complete barakhadi exercises

**Resources:** Barakhadi chart

**Checkpoint:** Can you read का कि की कु कू etc.?

---

### Day 6: Writing Practice
**Goals:**
- Solidify letter recognition
- Improve writing speed
- Practice reading simple words

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Complete [Writing Practice](../01-Alphabet/writing-practice.md)
- [ ] Practice reading simple words
- [ ] Write 20 words from the vocabulary list

**Resources:** Writing practice sheets

**Checkpoint:** Can you read simple Marathi words?

---

### Day 7: Week 1 Review
**Goals:**
- Review all alphabet content
- Identify weak areas
- Prepare for Week 2

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Review all vowels and consonants
- [ ] Retake alphabet quiz
- [ ] Practice barakhadi for all consonants
- [ ] Record a practice session for self-evaluation

**Weekly Checkpoint:** Can you read and write basic Marathi? Score at least 80% on the alphabet quiz.
