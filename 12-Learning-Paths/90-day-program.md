# 90-Day Comprehensive Marathi Program

A thorough, structured program taking you from complete beginner to intermediate proficiency. Includes all language skills: reading, writing, speaking, and listening, plus cultural context.

## 📋 Program Overview

| Month | Focus | Skills Developed |
|-------|-------|------------------|
| Month 1 | Foundation | Script mastery, basic grammar, essential vocabulary | 
| Month 2 | Building | Complex grammar, expanded vocabulary, conversations |
| Month 3 | Fluency | Reading comprehension, writing skills, cultural fluency |

**Daily Time Commitment:** 1-2 hours
**Weekly Structure:** 6 days learning + 1 day review

---

## Month 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

#### Day 1-2: Introduction & Vowels
**Goals:**
- Understand Devanagari script origin and structure
- Master all 12 vowels with perfect pronunciation
- Learn basic writing strokes

**Tasks:**
