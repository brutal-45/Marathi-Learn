# Sentence Structure in Marathi | मराठीतील वाक्य रचना

## Overview | सारांश

Sentence structure is the backbone of any language. This module covers how to construct sentences in Marathi, including word order, questions, and negation. Understanding these concepts will help you communicate effectively in Marathi.

वाक्य रचना हा कोणत्याही भाषेचा कणा आहे. या विभागात मराठीत वाक्य कसे बनवायचे, यात शब्दक्रम, प्रश्न आणि नकार यांचा समावेश आहे. हे संकल्पना समजून घेतल्यास तुम्ही मराठीत प्रभावीपणे संवाद साधू शकता.

---

## Learning Objectives | शिकण्याचे उद्दिष्ट्य

By completing this module, you will be able to:

- Understand and apply SOV word order
- Construct simple and complex sentences
- Form yes/no and wh-questions
- Use question words correctly
- Express negation in different tenses
- Create negative imperatives
- Transform sentences between forms

## Quick Reference | झटपट संदर्भ

### Word Order | शब्दक्रम

```
English (SVO): I eat rice
Marathi (SOV): मी भात खातो (I rice eat)
```

### Questions | प्रश्न

| Type | Example | English |
|------|---------|---------|
| Yes/No | तो जातो का? | Does he go? |
| Who? | कोण आहे? | Who is it? |
| What? | काय करता? | What do you do? |
| Where? | कुठे जाता? | Where do you go? |
| When? | कधी येणार? | When will you come? |
| How? | कसे आहात? | How are you? |
| Why? | का आलास? | Why did you come? |
| How much? | किती वाजले? | What time is it? |

### Negation | नकार

| Affirmative | Negative | English |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| जातो | जात नाही | doesn't go |
| गेलो | गेलो नाही | didn't go |
| जाईन | जाणार नाही | won't go |
| जा (command) | मत जा | don't go |

## Study Tips | अभ्यासाच्या टिप्स

1. **Master SOV First**: The verb-at-end pattern is fundamental. Practice until it becomes natural.

2. **Practice Questions Daily**: Ask yourself questions throughout the day in Marathi.

3. **Use Negation in Context**: Practice saying what you don't do, didn't do, won't do.

4. **Compare with English**: Understanding the differences helps reinforce learning.

5. **Write Sentences**: Create your own sentences using new patterns.

## Common Mistakes | सामान्य चुका

| Mistake | Correct | Explanation |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| मी खातो भात | मी भात खातो | Object before verb |
| तो काय आहे? | तो कोण आहे? | Use कोण for people |
| मी नाही जातो | मी जात नाही | नाही after verb stem |
| जा नाही | मत जा | Use मत for commands |
