# Noun Exercises | नामांचे सराव

## Introduction | परिचय

This exercise section is designed to help you practice and reinforce your understanding of Marathi nouns, including gender identification and singular-plural formation. Work through each section systematically to build your skills.

हा सराव विभाग तुम्हाला मराठी नामांची संकल्पना, लिंग ओळखणे आणि एकवचन-बहुवचन बनवणे यांचा सराव करण्यास मदत करेल. तुमचे कौशल्य वाढवण्यासाठी प्रत्येक विभागातून पद्धतशीरपणे सराव करा.

### A2. Gender by Word Ending

Based on the word ending, predict the likely gender:

| # | Word | Ending | Likely Gender |
|---|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | बाटली | -ई | ___ |
| 2 | रस्ता | -आ | ___ |
| 3 | पुस्तके | -ए | ___ |
| 4 | शिक्षक | Consonant | ___ |
| 5 | नाते | -ए | ___ |
| 6 | काठी | -ई | ___ |
| 7 | देश | Consonant | ___ |
| 8 | भाषा | -आ | ___ |
| 9 | फळ | Consonant | ___ |
| 10 | म्हैस | Consonant | ___ |

**Answer Key A2:**
1. F (words ending in -ई are typically feminine)
2. M (words ending in -आ are typically masculine)
3. N (words ending in -ए are typically neuter)
4. M (typically masculine for occupations)
5. N (words ending in -ए are typically neuter)
6. F (words ending in -ई are typically feminine)
7. M (typically masculine)
8. F (exception - words ending in -आ can be feminine too!)
9. N (fruit names are typically neuter)
10. F (female animal)
