# Nouns in Marathi | मराठीतील नाम

## Introduction | परिचय

Nouns (नाम - nām) are words that name people, places, things, animals, or ideas. In Marathi, nouns are one of the most important parts of speech because they form the foundation for sentence construction. Understanding nouns thoroughly will help you master other aspects of Marathi grammar.

नाम हे व्यक्ती, स्थान, वस्तू, प्राणी किंवा कल्पनांचे नाव दर्शविणारे शब्द आहेत. मराठीत, नाम हा वाक्यरचनेचा पाया आहे कारण ते इतर व्याकरणाच्या बाबींवर परिणाम करतात.

## Key Concepts in Marathi Nouns | मराठी नामांच्या महत्त्वाच्या संकल्पना

Marathi nouns have three important grammatical features:

### 1. Gender (लिंग - liṅga)
Marathi has three grammatical genders:
- **Masculine (पुल्लिंग - pulliṅga)** - Male beings and certain objects
- **Feminine (स्त्रीलिंग - strīliṅga)** - Female beings and certain objects
- **Neuter (नपुंसकलिंग - napuṃsakaliṅga)** - Inanimate objects and abstract concepts

📖 **Detailed Guide**: [gender.md](./gender.md)

### 2. Number (वचन - vacana)
Nouns change form based on quantity:
- **Singular (एकवचन - ekavacana)** - One item
- **Plural (बहुवचन - bahuvacana)** - Multiple items

📖 **Detailed Guide**: [singular-plural.md](./singular-plural.md)

### 3. Case (विभक्ती - vibhaktī)
Nouns change form based on their role in the sentence. There are eight cases in Marathi.

## Types of Nouns | नामांचे प्रकार

### By Nature | स्वभावानुसार

| Type | Marathi | Description | Example |
|------|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Common Noun** | सामान्य नाम | General names for persons, places, things | मुलगा (boy), पुस्तक (book) |
| **Proper Noun** | विशेष नाम | Specific names of persons, places | मुंबई (Mumbai), राम (Ram) |
| **Collective Noun** | समूहवाचक नाम | Groups of things or people | टोळी (gang), गट (group) |
| **Abstract Noun** | भाववाचक नाम | Ideas, qualities, feelings | प्रेम (love), सत्य (truth) |
| **Material Noun** | द्रव्यवाचक नाम | Substances or materials | पाणी (water), सोने (gold) |

## Gender Quick Reference | लिंगाचा झटपट संदर्भ

| Gender | Marathi | Typical Endings | Examples |
|--------|---------|-----------------|----------|
| **Masculine** | पुल्लिंग | -आ (-ā), -क (-ka) | मुलगा (boy), रस्ता (road) |
| **Feminine** | स्त्रीलिंग | -ई (-ī), -न (-na) | मुलगी (girl), आई (mother) |
| **Neuter** | नपुंसकलिंग | -ं (-ṃ), -त (-ta) | पुस्तक (book), घर (house) |

## Singular-Plural Quick Reference | एकवचन-बहुवचन झटपट संदर्भ

| Gender | Singular | Plural | Pattern |
|--------|----------|--------|---------|
| Masculine | मुलगा (mulgā) | मुले (mulē) | -ā → -ē |
| Feminine | मुलगी (mulgī) | मुली (mulī) | -ī → -ī (shortened) |
| Neuter | पुस्तक (pustak) | पुस्तके (pustakē) | adds -ē |

## Why Nouns Matter | नाम का महत्त्वाचे आहे?

In Marathi, nouns affect:

1. **Adjective Agreement** | विशेषणाचे सामंजस्य
   - The adjective must match the noun's gender and number
   - Example: छान मुलगा (nice boy) → छान मुलगी (nice girl)

2. **Verb Agreement** | क्रियापदाचे सामंजस्य
   - The verb must agree with the subject noun
   - Example: मुलगा खेळतो (boy plays) → मुलगी खेळते (girl plays)

3. **Pronoun Replacement** | सर्वनाम बदल
   - Pronouns must match the noun's gender
   - Example: मुलगा → तो (he), मुलगी → ती (she)

## Tips for Learning Nouns | नाम शिकण्यासाठी टिप्स

1. **Learn Gender with the Noun** | नामासोबत लिंग शिका
   - Always learn the gender when learning a new noun
   - Example: Don't just learn "पुस्तक" - learn "पुस्तक (नपुंसकलिंग)"

2. **Group by Patterns** | पद्धतीनुसार गट करा
   - Notice patterns in noun endings
   - Most nouns ending in -आ are masculine
   - Most nouns ending in -ई are feminine

3. **Practice with Sentences** | वाक्यांसह सराव करा
   - Use nouns in complete sentences
   - This helps with adjective and verb agreement

4. **Create Flashcards** | फ्लॅशकार्ड तयार करा
   - Front: English word
   - Back: Marathi word with gender and transliteration
