## 13. Translation Practice | अनुवाद सराव

### Exercise 13.1: English to Marathi | इंग्रजी ते मराठी

Translate the following sentences to Marathi:

1. I go to school every day. (male)
2. She is reading a book.
3. They went to Mumbai yesterday.
4. We will come tomorrow.
5. Please sit here.
6. Don't go there!
7. He was sleeping.
8. Let's eat together.
9. She doesn't speak Marathi.
10. I will not go there.

<details>
<summary>📝 Click for Answers | उत्तरांसाठी क्लिक करा</summary>

1. I go to school every day. → **मी रोज शाळेत जातो.** (male)
2. She is reading a book. → **ती पुस्तक वाचत आहे.**
3. They went to Mumbai yesterday. → **ते काल मुंबईला गेली.**
4. We will come tomorrow. → **आम्ही उद्या येऊ.**
5. Please sit here. → **कृपया इथे बसा.**
6. Don't go there! → **तिथे मत जा!** (informal) / **तिथे जाऊ नका!** (formal)
7. He was sleeping. → **तो झोपत होता.**
8. Let's eat together. → **चला, एकत्र खाऊया!**
9. She doesn't speak Marathi. → **ती मराठी बोलत नाही.**
10. I will not go there. → **मी तिथे जाणार नाही.**

</details>

## 📚 Key Takeaways | मुख्य मुद्दे

1. **Practice each tense separately before mixing**
   **मिसळण्यापूर्वी प्रत्येक काळाचा स्वतंत्रपणे सराव करा**

2. **Pay attention to gender and number**
   **लिंग आणि वचनाकडे लक्ष द्या**

3. **Memorize irregular verb forms**
   **अनियमित क्रियापद रूपे लक्षात ठेवा**

4. **Use transliteration as a guide for pronunciation**
   **उच्चारासाठी ट्रान्सलिटरेशनचा मार्गदर्शक म्हणून वापर करा**

5. **Practice with native speakers when possible**
   **जेव्हा शक्य असेल तेव्हा मूळ भाषकांसोबत सराव करा**
