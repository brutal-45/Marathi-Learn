# Common Postpositions in Marathi | मराठीतील सामान्य शब्दयोगी अव्यय

## Introduction | परिचय

Postpositions (शब्दयोगी अव्यय - śabdyogī avyay) are words that show the relationship between a noun and other words in a sentence. Unlike English, which uses prepositions (words that come BEFORE nouns), Marathi uses postpositions - words that come AFTER nouns. This is a fundamental difference between the two languages.

शब्दयोगी अव्यय हे वाक्यातील नाम आणि इतर शब्दांमधील संबंध दर्शवणारे शब्द आहेत. इंग्रजीप्रमाणे, जे नामांपूर्वी येणारे शब्द (prepositions) वापरते, मराठीत नामांनंतर येणारे शब्द (postpositions) वापरले जातात. हा दोन भाषांमधील मूलभूत फरक आहे.

## Prepositions vs Postpositions | पूर्वसर्ग विरुद्ध उत्तरसर्ग

| English | Marathi |
|---------|---------|
| **IN** the house | घरा**मध्ये** (gharā**madhyē**) |
| **ON** the table | टेबला**वर** (ṭēbalā**var**) |
| **UNDER** the tree | झाडा**खाली** (jhāḍā**khālī**) |
| **FROM** Pune | पुण्या**पासून** (puṇyā**pāsūn**) |
| **TO** Mumbai | मुंबई**ला** (mumbaī**lā**) |

Notice how the English preposition comes BEFORE the noun, while the Marathi postposition comes AFTER the noun!

## 1. मध्ये (madhyē) - In, Inside | मध्ये

### Usage | वापर

**मध्ये** indicates location inside something - corresponding to "in", "inside", or "within" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| in the house | घरामध्ये | gharāmadhyē |
| in the school | शाळेमध्ये | śāḷēmadhyē |
| in the water | पाण्यामध्ये | pāṇyāmadhyē |
| in the box | डब्यामध्ये | ḍabyāmadhyē |
| in the city | शहरामध्ये | śaharāmadhyē |
| in the book | पुस्तकामध्ये | pustakāmadhyē |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| मूल घरामध्ये आहे. | mūla gharamadhyē āhē. | The child is in the house. |
| पाण्यामध्ये मासे आहेत. | pāṇyāmadhyē māsē āhēt. | There are fish in the water. |
| पुस्तकामध्ये चित्रे आहेत. | pustakāmadhyē citrē āhēt. | There are pictures in the book. |
| तो शहरामध्ये राहतो. | tō śaharāmadhyē rāhatō. | He lives in the city. |

---

## 2. वर (var) - On, Upon, Above | वर

### Usage | वापर

**वर** indicates position on a surface or above something - corresponding to "on", "upon", or "above" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| on the table | टेबलवर | ṭēbalavar |
| on the chair | खुर्चीवर | khurcīvar |
| on the wall | भिंतीवर | bhintīvar |
| on the head | डोक्यावर | ḍōkyāvar |
| on the road | रस्त्यावर | rastyāvar |
| on the paper | कागदावर | kāgadāvar |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| पुस्तक टेबलवर आहे. | pustak ṭēbalavar āhē. | The book is on the table. |
| पक्षी झाडावर बसला आहे. | pakṣī jhāḍāvar basalā āhē. | The bird is sitting on the tree. |
| चित्र भिंतीवर आहे. | citra bhintīvar āhē. | The picture is on the wall. |
| सूर्य आकाशावर आहे. | sūrya ākāśāvar āhē. | The sun is in the sky. |

---

## 3. खाली (khālī) - Under, Below | खाली

### Usage | वापर

**खाली** indicates position below something - corresponding to "under", "below", or "beneath" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| under the tree | झाडाखाली | jhāḍākhālī |
| under the table | टेबलखाली | ṭēbalkhālī |
| under the bed | पलंगाखाली | palaṅgākhālī |
| under the sky | आकाशाखाली | ākāśākhālī |
| below the knee | गुडघ्याखाली | guḍghyākhālī |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| कुत्रा झाडाखाली आहे. | kutrā jhāḍākhālī āhē. | The dog is under the tree. |
| चप्पल पलंगाखाली आहे. | cappal palaṅgākhālī āhē. | The slippers are under the bed. |
| मुले झाडाखाली खेळत आहेत. | mulē jhāḍākhālī khēḷat āhēt. | Children are playing under the tree. |

---

## 4. पासून (pāsūn) - From | पासून

### Usage | वापर

**पासून** indicates origin or source - corresponding to "from" in English. It's used for:
- Starting point of movement
- Source or origin
- Cause or reason

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| from home | घरून / घरापासून | gharūn / gharāpāsūn |
| from Mumbai | मुंबईपासून | mumbaīpāsūn |
| from the beginning | सुरुवातीपासून | suruvātīpāsūn |
| from childhood | लहानपणापासून | lahānpaṇāpāsūn |
| from the sky | आकाशापासून | ākāśāpāsūn |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| तो पुण्यापासून आला. | tō puṇyāpāsūn ālā. | He came from Pune. |
| ही भेटवस्तू माझ्यापासून आहे. | hī bētvastū mājhyāpāsūn āhē. | This gift is from me. |
| तो लहानपणापासून हुशार आहे. | tō lahānpaṇāpāsūn huśār āhē. | He has been intelligent from childhood. |

---

## 5. पर्यंत (paryant) - Until, Up to, As far as | पर्यंत

### Usage | वापर

**पर्यंत** indicates limit or extent - corresponding to "until", "up to", "as far as", or "till" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| until tomorrow | उद्यापर्यंत | udyāparyant |
| up to the market | बाजारापर्यंत | bājārāparyant |
| as far as the river | नदीपर्यंत | nadīparyant |
| till evening | संध्याकाळपर्यंत | saṅdhyākāḷparyant |
| up to the end | शेवटपर्यंत | śēvaṭparyant |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| मी उद्यापर्यंत थांबेन. | mī udyāparyant thāṃbēn. | I will stay until tomorrow. |
| तो बाजारापर्यंत गेला. | tō bājārāparyant gēlā. | He went up to the market. |
| पाऊस संध्याकाळपर्यंत पडेल. | pāusa saṅdhyākāḷparyant paḍēl. | It will rain until evening. |

---

## 6. सोबत (sōbat) - With, Along with | सोबत

### Usage | वापर

**सोबत** indicates accompaniment - corresponding to "with" or "along with" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| with me | माझ्यासोबत | mājhyāsōbat |
| with friends | मित्रांसोबत | mitrāṃsōbat |
| with family | कुटुंबासोबत | kuṭuṃbāsōbat |
| with him | त्याच्यासोबत | tyācyāsōbat |
| with happiness | आनंदासोबत | ānandāsōbat |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| तो माझ्यासोबत येईल. | tō mājhyāsōbat yēīl. | He will come with me. |
| मी मित्रांसोबत खेळतो. | mī mitrāṃsōbat khēḷatō. | I play with friends. |
| ती आईसोबत बोलते. | tī āīsōbat bōlatē. | She talks with her mother. |
| आनंदासोबत जगा. | ānandāsōbat jagā. | Live with happiness. |

---

## 7. साठी (sāṭhī) - For | साठी

### Usage | वापर

**साठी** indicates purpose or beneficiary - corresponding to "for" in English.

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| for me | माझ्यासाठी | mājhyāsāṭhī |
| for you | तुमच्यासाठी | tumcyāsāṭhī |
| for the country | देशासाठी | dēśāsāṭhī |
| for study | अभ्यासासाठी | abhyāsāsāṭhī |
| for sale | विक्रीसाठी | vikrīsāṭhī |
| for two days | दोन दिवसांसाठी | dōn divasāṃsāṭhī |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| हे पुस्तक तुमच्यासाठी आहे. | hē pustak tumcyāsāṭhī āhē. | This book is for you. |
| मी देशासाठी काम करतो. | mī dēśāsāṭhī kām karatō. | I work for the country. |
| हे जेवण माझ्यासाठी आहे. | hē jevaṇ mājhyāsāṭhī āhē. | This food is for me. |

## 8. ला (lā) - To | ला

### Usage | वापर

**ला** is a very versatile postposition used for:
- Direction (to)
- Indirect object marker
- Time expressions
- Possession (with certain constructions)

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| to Mumbai | मुंबईला | mumbaīlā |
| to school | शाळेला | śāḷēlā |
| to the boy | मुलाला | mulālā |
| at 5 o'clock | पाच वाजता | pāc vājatā |
| to me | मला | malā |
| to him | त्याला | tyālā |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| तो मुंबईला जातो. | tō mumbaīlā jātō. | He goes to Mumbai. |
| मला आंबे आवडतात. | malā āṃbē avadtāt. | I like mangoes. |
| त्याला पैसे द्या. | tyālā paīsē dyā. | Give money to him. |
| मी सकाळी शाळेला जातो. | mī sakāḷī śāḷēlā jātō. | I go to school in the morning. |

---

## 9. चा/ची/चे (cā/cī/cē) - Of, 's | चा/ची/चे

### Usage | वापर

This is a genitive postposition showing possession or relationship. It changes form based on the gender of the following noun:

| Gender | Form | Example |
|--------|------|---------|
| Masculine | चा (cā) | रामचा मुलगा (Ram's son) |
| Feminine | ची (cī) | रामची बहीण (Ram's sister) |
| Neuter | चे (cē) | रामचे घर (Ram's house) |
| Plural | ची (cī) | रामची पुस्तके (Ram's books) |

### Examples | उदाहरणे

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Ram's book | रामचे पुस्तक | rāmcē pustak |
| Sita's house | सीतेचे घर | sītēcē ghar |
| My mother | माझी आई | mājhī āī |
| Your name | तुमचे नाव | tumcē nāv |
| The color of the sky | आकाशाचा रंग | ākāśācā raṅga |

---

## 10. ने (nē) - By, With (instrument) | ने

### Usage | वापर

**ने** indicates:
- Agent in passive constructions
- Instrument (means by which something is done)
- Time duration

| English | Marathi | Transliteration |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| by Ram | रामने | rāmnē |
| with a pen | पेनाने | pēnānē |
| by hand | हाताने | hātānē |
| by bus | बसने | basnē |
| in two days | दोन दिवसांनी | dōn divasāṃnī |

### Example Sentences | उदाहरण वाक्ये

| Marathi | Transliteration | English |
|---------|-----------------|---------|
| रामने पुस्तक लिहिले. | rāmnē pustak lihilē. | Ram wrote the book. |
| मी पेनाने लिहितो. | mī pēnānē lihitō. | I write with a pen. |
| तो बसने जातो. | tō basnē jātō. | He goes by bus. |

## Summary | सारांश

| Key Point | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Position** | Postpositions come AFTER the noun |
| **Form Changes** | Some postpositions change the noun form |
| **Common ones** | मध्ये, वर, खाली, पासून, पर्यंत, सोबत, साठी |
| **Genitive** | चा/ची/चे changes with following noun's gender |
| **Direction** | ला marks direction and indirect objects |
