# Place Adverbs | स्थानवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

Place adverbs indicate **where** an action takes place. They answer the question "कुठे?" (Where?).

## Common Place Adverbs | सामान्य स्थानवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

### Basic Location | मूळ स्थान

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Context |
|---------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| here | इथे | ithe | Near speaker |
| there | तिथे | tithe | Away from speaker |
| where | कुठे | kuṭhe | Question |
| somewhere | कुठेतरी | kuṭhetarī | Indefinite location |
| everywhere | सर्वत्र | sarvatr | All places |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **इथे बस.**
   - *Ithe bas.*
   - Sit here.

2. **तिथे काय आहे?**
   - *Tithe kāy āhe?*
   - What is there?

3. **कुठे जात आहात?**
   - *Kuṭhe jāt āhāt?*
   - Where are you going?

4. **पैसे कुठेतरी हरवले.**
   - *Paise kuṭhetarī haravale.*
   - The money is lost somewhere.

5. **देव सर्वत्र आहे.**
   - *Dev sarvatr āhe.*
   - God is everywhere.

### Direction | दिशा

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| up/above | वर | var | Higher position |
| down/below | खाली | khālī | Lower position |
| inside | आत | āt | Enclosed space |
| outside | बाहेर | bāher | Not enclosed |
| forward | पुढे | puḍhe | Ahead |
| backward | मागे | māge | Behind |
| nearby | जवळ | javaḷ | Close |
| far | लांब | lāmb | Distant |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **वर पहा.**
   - *Var pahā.*
   - Look up.

2. **खाली उतर.**
   - *Khālī utar.*
   - Come down.

3. **आत ये.**
   - *Āt ye.*
   - Come inside.

4. **बाहेर जा.**
   - *Bāher jā.*
   - Go outside.

5. **पुढे चल.**
   - *Puḍhe cal.*
   - Move forward.

6. **मागे वळ.**
   - *Māge vaḷ.*
   - Turn back.

7. **घर जवळ आहे.**
   - *Ghar javaḷ āhe.*
   - The house is nearby.

8. **गाव खूप लांब आहे.**
   - *Gāv khūp lāmb āhe.*
   - The village is very far.

### Relative Place | सापेक्ष स्थान

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Meaning |
|---------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| near | जवळपास | javaḷpās | Close to |
| far away | लांबच लांब | lāmbac lāmb | Very far |
| around | सभोवताली | sabhovtālī | Surrounding |
| across | समोर | samor | Opposite side |
| next to | शेजारी | śejārī | Adjacent |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **शाळा जवळपास आहे.**
   - *Śālā javaḷpās āhe.*
   - The school is nearby.

2. **समोर काय आहे?**
   - *Samor kāy āhe?*
   - What is across/opposite?

3. **शेजारी कोण राहतो?**
   - *Śejārī koṇ rāhto?*
   - Who lives next door?

## Demonstrative Place Adverbs | द्योतक स्थानविशेषण

| Near Speaker | Away from Speaker | Question |
|--------------|-------------------|----------|
| इथे (here) | तिथे (there) | कुठे (where) |
| इथून (from here) | तिथून (from there) | कुठून (from where) |
| इथे पर्यंत (up to here) | तिथे पर्यंत (up to there) | कुठे पर्यंत (up to where) |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **इथून निघ.**
   - *Ithūn nigh.*
   - Leave from here.

2. **तिथून घेऊन ये.**
   - *Tithūn gheūn ye.*
   - Bring it from there.

3. **कुठून आलास?**
   - *Kuṭhūn ālās?*
   - Where did you come from?

## Practice Sentences | सराव वाक्ये

**Translate to Marathi:**

1. The book is here. → **पुस्तक इथे आहे.**
2. Go outside and play. → **बाहेर जाऊन खेळ.**
3. Where is my bag? → **माझे साठा कुठे आहे?**
4. Look down! → **खाली पहा!**
5. Come inside the house. → **घरात आत ये.**
