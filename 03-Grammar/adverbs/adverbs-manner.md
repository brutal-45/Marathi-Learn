# Manner Adverbs | रीतिवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

Manner adverbs describe **how** an action is performed. They answer the question "कसे?" (How?).

## Common Manner Adverbs | सामान्य रीतिवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

### Speed | वेग

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| slowly | हळू | hāḷū | Gentle pace |
| quickly/fast | लवकर / वेगाने | lavkar / vegāne | Fast pace |
| hurriedly | घाईघाईने | ghāīghāīne | In a rush |
| gradually | हळूहळू | hāḷūhāḷū | Step by step |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **हळू बोल.**
   - *Hāḷū bol.*
   - Speak slowly.

2. **वेगाने धाव.**
   - *Vegāne dhāv.*
   - Run fast.

3. **घाईघाईने जेवू नको.**
   - *Ghāīghāīne jevū nako.*
   - Don't eat in a hurry.

4. **हळूहळू शिक.**
   - *Hāḷūhāḷū śik.*
   - Learn gradually.

### Quality | गुणवत्ता

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| properly/neatly | नीट | nīṭ | Correct way |
| correctly | बरोबर | barobar | Accurate |
| wrongly | चुकून | cukūn | Incorrectly |
| carefully | काळजीपूर्वक | kāḷjīpūrvak | With care |
| nicely | छान | chān | Pleasantly |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **नीट लिही.**
   - *Nīṭ lihī.*
   - Write properly.

2. **बरोबर उत्तर दे.**
   - *Barobar uttar de.*
   - Give the correct answer.

3. **चुकून चाललो.**
   - *Cukūn cāllo.*
   - Went the wrong way.

4. **काळजीपूर्वक वाच.**
   - *Kāḷjīpūrvak vāc.*
   - Read carefully.

5. **छान गातोस.**
   - *Chān gātos.*
   - You sing nicely.

### Attitude | वृत्ती

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| lovingly | प्रेमाने | premāne | With love |
| angrily | रागाने | rāgāne | With anger |
| happily | आनंदाने | ānandāne | With joy |
| sadly | दुःखाने | duḥkhāne | With sorrow |
| willingly | आनंदाने / इच्छेने | iccheṇe | With willingness |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **आई प्रेमाने बोलते.**
   - *Āī premāne bolt.*
   - Mother speaks lovingly.

2. **रागाने बोलू नको.**
   - *Rāgāne bolū nako.*
   - Don't speak angrily.

3. **आनंदाने खेळ.**
   - *Ānandāne kheḷ.*
   - Play happily.

4. **तो दुःखाने म्हणाला.**
   - *To duḥkhāne mhaṇālā.*
   - He said sadly.

### Formation from Adjectives | विशेषणांपासून तयारी

Many manner adverbs are formed by adding suffixes to adjectives:

| Adjective | Suffix | Adverb |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| चांगला (good) | -पणे | चांगलेपणे (well) |
| वेगवान (fast) | -ाने | वेगाने (quickly) |
| सुंदर (beautiful) | -रित्या | सुंदररित्या (beautifully) |
| सावध (careful) | -पणे | सावधपणे (carefully) |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **तो चांगलेपणे वागतो.**
   - *To cāṅgalepaṇe vāgto.*
   - He behaves well.

2. **सुंदररित्या लिही.**
   - *Sundararityā lihī.*
   - Write beautifully.

3. **सावधपणे चल.**
   - *Sāvadhdpaṇe cal.*
   - Walk carefully.

## Special Manner Adverbs | विशेष रीतिवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Meaning |
|---------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| together | एकत्र | ekatra | In a group |
| alone | एकटा | ekaṭā | By oneself |
| secretly | गुपचूप | gupacūp | Hidden |
| openly | उघडपणे | ughaḍpaṇe | Not hidden |
| specially | विशेष | viśeṣ | Particularly |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **आपण एकत्र जाऊया.**
   - *Āpaṇ ekatra jāūyā.*
   - Let's go together.

2. **तो एकटा राहतो.**
   - *To ekaṭā rāhto.*
   - He lives alone.

3. **गुपचूप निघून गेला.**
   - *Gupacūp nighūn gelā.*
   - Left secretly.

## Practice Sentences | सराव वाक्ये

**Translate to Marathi:**

1. Walk slowly. → **हळू चल.**
2. Read carefully. → **काळजीपूर्वक वाच.**
3. She sings beautifully. → **ती सुंदर गाते.**
4. Don't speak angrily. → **रागाने बोलू नको.**
5. Work properly. → **नीट काम कर.**
