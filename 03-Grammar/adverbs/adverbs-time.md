# Time Adverbs | कालवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

Time adverbs indicate **when** an action takes place. They answer the question "कधी?" (When?).

## Common Time Adverbs | सामान्य कालवाचक क्रियाविशेषण

### Present Time | वर्तमान काळ

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| now | आता | ātā | Present moment |
| today | आज | āj | Current day |
| right now | आत्ताच | āttāc | Immediate action |
| just now | आता आता | ātā ātā | Very recent |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **आता जा.**
   - *Ātā jā.*
   - Go now.

2. **आज पाऊस पडत आहे.**
   - *Āj pāus paḍat āhe.*
   - It is raining today.

3. **आत्ताच बघ!**
   - *Āttāc bagh!*
   - Look right now!

### Past Time | भूतकाळ

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| then | मग | mag | Sequence |
| later | नंतर | nantar | After some time |
| yesterday | काल | kāl | Previous day |
| before | आधी | ādhī | Earlier |
| recently |अलीकडे | alīkaḍe | Near past |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **मग तो आला.**
   - *Mag to ālā.*
   - Then he came.

2. **नंतर भेटू.**
   - *Nantar bheṭū.*
   - We'll meet later.

3. **आधी जेव नंतर जा.**
   - *Ādhī jev nantar jā.*
   - Eat first, then go.

4. **काल मी पुण्याला होतो.**
   - *Kāl mī puṇyālā hoto.*
   - I was in Pune yesterday.

### Future Time | भविष्यकाळ

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Usage |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| tomorrow | उद्या | udyā | Next day |
| day after tomorrow | परवा | parvā | Two days later |
| soon | लवकर | lavkar | Near future |
| sometime | कधीतरी | kadhītarī | Indefinite future |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **उद्या येईन.**
   - *Udyā yeīn.*
   - I will come tomorrow.

2. **लवकर ये!**
   - *Lavkar ye!*
   - Come soon!

3. **कधीतरी भेटूया.**
   - *Kadhītarī bheṭūyā.*
   - Let's meet sometime.

### Indefinite Time | अनिश्चित काळ

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Meaning |
|---------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| always | नेहमी | nehmi | Every time |
| never | कधीही नाही | kadhīhī nāhī | At no time |
| sometimes | कधीकधी | kadhīkadhī | Occasionally |
| anytime | कधीही | kadhīhī | Any time |

**Examples | उदाहरणे:**

1. **तो नेहमी उशिरा येतो.**
   - *To nehmi uśīrā yeto.*
   - He always comes late.

2. **मी कधीही नाही जात.**
   - *Mī kadhīhī nāhī jāt.*
   - I never go.

3. **कधीकधी चित्रपट पाहतो.**
   - *Kadhīkadhī citrapāṭ pāhto.*
   - Sometimes I watch movies.

## Sentence Structure | वाक्य रचना

Time adverbs can appear at different positions:

### Beginning Position (Emphasis)
```
आता मी जातो. = Now I go.
आज शाळा नाही. = Today, no school.
```

### Before Verb (Neutral)
```
मी आता जातो. = I go now.
तो नेहमी येतो. = He always comes.
```

## Practice Sentences | सराव वाक्ये

Translate the following:

1. Come tomorrow. → **उद्या ये.**
2. I will do it later. → **मी ते नंतर करेन.**
3. She always speaks the truth. → **ती नेहमी सत्य बोलते.**
4. Don't go now. → **आता जाऊ नकोस.**
