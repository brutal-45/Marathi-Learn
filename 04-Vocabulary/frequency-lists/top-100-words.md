## 📊 Word Frequency Groups

### Top 25 (Essential)
Words 1-25 are the most essential - learn these first! They include pronouns, question words, and basic connectors.

### Words 26-50 (Numbers & Adjectives)
These include numbers, quantities, and descriptive words.

### Words 51-75 (Verbs & Time)
Common verbs and time-related vocabulary.

### Words 76-100 (Nouns)
Essential nouns for everyday objects and people.

## 💡 Study Tips for Frequency Lists

1. **Learn in groups of 10** - Master each group before moving on
2. **Create flashcards** - Write English on one side, Marathi on the other
3. **Use in sentences** - Practice making your own sentences
4. **Daily review** - Spend 10 minutes daily reviewing

## 🔄 Practice Exercise

Try to translate these sentences using words from the list above:

1. I want water.
2. Where is the school?
3. What is your name?
4. This house is big.
5. I don't know.
