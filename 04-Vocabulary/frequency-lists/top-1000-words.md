## 💡 Study Tips

1. **Learn 10 words daily** - Consistent small progress
2. **Use flashcards** - Anki or physical cards
3. **Write sentences** - Practice in context
4. **Group by category** - Learn related words together
5. **Review weekly** - Spaced repetition works
6. **Listen to native speakers** - Hear words in natural context
7. **Read simple texts** - Children's books, news articles
8. **Practice speaking** - Use new words in conversation
