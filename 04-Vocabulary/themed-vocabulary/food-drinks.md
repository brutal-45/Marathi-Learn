# Food and Drinks - अन्न आणि पेये

Food and drinks vocabulary in Marathi.

## Basic Foods

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Rice | भात | bhāta | आज भात आणि डाळ आहे. (Today there is rice and dal.) |
| Bread | रोटी | roṭī | रोटी खूप गरम आहे. (The bread is very hot.) |
| Dal (lentils) | डाळ | ḍāḷ | डाळ चविष्ट आहे. (The dal is tasty.) |
| Vegetable | भाजी | bhājī | भाजी ताजी आहे. (The vegetable is fresh.) |
| Curry | साल | sāla | चिकन साल चांगले आहे. (The chicken curry is good.) |

## Usage Notes

- **भात (bhāta)** is the staple food of Maharashtra
- **चहा (cahā)** is an essential part of Marathi daily life
- **जेवण (jevaṇa)** refers to a complete meal
- Food vocabulary often relates to the taste adjective changes
