| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|

## Usage Notes

- Many occupation words are borrowed from English (डॉक्टर, मॅनेजर)
- Traditional occupations have specific Marathi terms (सुतार, लोहार, न्हावी)
- Gender-specific forms exist: शिक्षक (male teacher) / शिक्षिका (female teacher)
- **व्यवसाय (vyavasāya)** means occupation or profession
