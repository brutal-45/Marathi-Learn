## Usage Notes

- Most technology terms are borrowed from English and written in Marathi script
- **संगणक (saṅgaṇaka)** is the traditional Marathi word for computer
- **तंत्रज्ञान (taṃtrajñāna)** means technology
- English abbreviations like Wi-Fi, URL, GPS are commonly used as-is
- Many verbs in technology are formed by adding करणे (karaṇē - to do) to English words
