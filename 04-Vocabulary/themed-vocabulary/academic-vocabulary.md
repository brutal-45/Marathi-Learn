## Usage Notes

- **शिक्षण (śikṣaṇa)** means education
- **अभ्यास (abhyāsa)** means study
- Gender-specific forms exist: शिक्षक (male teacher) / शिक्षिका (female teacher), विद्यार्थी (male student) / विद्यार्थिनी (female student)
- Many technical terms are borrowed from Sanskrit
- Academic year in Maharashtra typically runs from June to April
