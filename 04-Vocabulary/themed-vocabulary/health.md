# Health - आरोग्य

Health and medical vocabulary in Marathi.

## Basic Health Terms

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Health | आरोग्य | ārogya | आरोग्य चांगले आहे. (Health is good.) |
| Body | शरीर | śarīra | शरीर निरोगी ठेवा. (Keep body healthy.) |
| Life | जीवन | jīvana | निरोगी जीवन जगा. (Live a healthy life.) |
| Disease | रोग | roga | रोग टाळा. (Avoid disease.) |
| Illness | आजार | ājāra | आजार बरा झाला. (Illness is cured.) |
| Patient | रुग्ण | rugṇa | रुग्ण आहे. (Patient is there.) |

## Usage Notes

- **आरोग्य (ārogya)** is the formal term for health
- **आजार (ājāra)** refers to illness, **रोग (roga)** refers to disease
- **डॉक्टर (ḍŏkṭara)** is commonly used for doctor
- **औषध (auṣadha)** means medicine in general
