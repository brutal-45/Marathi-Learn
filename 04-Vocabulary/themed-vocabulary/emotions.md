## Usage Notes

- **भावना (bhāvanā)** means both "emotion" and "feeling"
- **वाटणे (vāṭaṇe)** is commonly used to express feelings: "मला ... वाटते" (I feel...)
- **आनंद (ānanda)** is a positive emotion, while **दुःख (duḥkha)** is sorrow
- **राग (rāga)** can mean anger or passion depending on context
- Emotions often use the neuter gender: **आनंद वाटतो** (happiness is felt)
