Body parts vocabulary in Marathi.

| Eye | डोळा | ḍoḷā | डोळे मोठे आहेत. (Eyes are big.) |
| Ear | कान | kāna | कान ऐकतात. (Ears hear.) |

| Tooth | दात | dāta | दात स्वच्छ आहेत. (Teeth are clean.) |
| Tongue | जीभ | jībha | जीभेने चव येते. (Taste comes from tongue.) |

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| To see | पाहणे | pāhaṇe | डोळ्यांनी पाहा. (See with eyes.) |
| To hear | ऐकणे | aikhaṇe | कानाने ऐका. (Hear with ears.) |
| To eat | खाणे | khāṇe | तोंडाने खा. (Eat with mouth.) |
| To walk | चालणे | cālaṇe | पायांनी चाला. (Walk with legs.) |

## Usage Notes

- **डोके (ḍoke)** is neuter gender
- **हात (hāta)** can mean both hand and arm
- **पाय (pāya)** can mean both leg and foot
- Body parts often used with verbs to describe actions
