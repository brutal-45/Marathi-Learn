## Usage Notes

- **कायदा (kāyadā)** refers to law in general
- **न्यायालय (nyāyālaya)** is the formal word for court
- **वकील (vakīla)** is commonly used for lawyer
- Many legal terms in Marathi come from Sanskrit origins
- Court proceedings in Maharashtra can be in Marathi, Hindi, or English
- **संविधान (saṃvidhāna)** refers specifically to the Constitution
