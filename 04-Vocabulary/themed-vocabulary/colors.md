# Colors - रंग

Colors and color-related vocabulary in Marathi.

## Basic Colors

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Red | लाल | lāl | गुलाब लाल आहे. (The rose is red.) |
| Blue | निळा | niḷā | आकाश निळे आहे. (The sky is blue.) |
| Green | हिरवा | hiravā | गवत हिरवे आहे. (The grass is green.) |
| White | पांढरा | pāṇḍharā | ढग पांढरे आहेत. (The clouds are white.) |
| Black | काळा | kāḷā | काळा कुत्रा आहे. (There is a black dog.) |

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |

| Orange | संत्रा | saṃtrā | संत्रा फळ संत्रे रंगाचे असते. (Orange fruit is orange in color.) |

## Color Adjectives Agreement

In Marathi, color adjectives change based on the gender of the noun:

| Color | Masculine | Feminine | Neuter |
|-------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Red | लाल (lāl) | लाल (lāl) | लाल (lāl) |
| Blue | निळा (niḷā) | निळी (niḷī) | निळे (niḷe) |
| Green | हिरवा (hiravā) | हिरवी (hiravī) | हिरवे (hirave) |
| White | पांढरा (pāṇḍharā) | पांढरी (pāṇḍharī) | पांढरे (pāṇḍhare) |
| Black | काळा (kāḷā) | काळी (kāḷī) | काळे (kāḷe) |
| Yellow | पिवळा (pivaḷā) | पिवळी (pivaḷī) | पिवळे (pivaḷe) |

## Usage Notes

- Colors in Marathi agree with the gender of the noun they describe
- **लाल (lāl)** remains the same for all genders
- Most color adjectives end in **-आ (ā)** for masculine, **-ई (ī)** for feminine, and **-ए (e)** for neuter
