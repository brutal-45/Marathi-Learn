# Shopping - खरेदी

Shopping vocabulary including market, stores, and transactions in Marathi.

## Basic Shopping Terms

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Shopping | खरेदी | kharedī | खरेदी करायला जाऊ. (Let's go shopping.) |
| Market | बाजार | bājāra | बाजारात गर्दी आहे. (There is a crowd in the market.) |
| Shop | दुकान | dukāna | दुकान उघडे आहे. (The shop is open.) |
| Store | दुकान | dukāna | मोठे दुकान आहे. (It's a big store.) |
| Mall | मॉल | mŏla | मॉलमध्ये चालूया. (Let's walk in the mall.) |

## Usage Notes

- **बाजार (bājāra)** refers to both traditional markets and the act of shopping
- **सूट (sūṭa)** means both discount and leave/holiday
- **दुकान (dukāna)** is used for any type of shop or store
- Prices are often negotiated in traditional markets
- **पैसे (paise)** is plural; singular **पैसा (paisā)** is one paisa
