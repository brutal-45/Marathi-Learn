## Usage Notes

- **व्यवसाय (vyavasāya)** refers to business or profession
- **व्यापार (vyāpāra)** specifically refers to trade
- English loanwords are common in modern business terminology
- Numbers and amounts in Marathi use the Indian numbering system (लाख, कोटी)
