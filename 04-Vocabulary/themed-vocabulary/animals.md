# Animals - प्राणी

Common animals and related vocabulary in Marathi.

## Domestic Animals

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Dog | कुत्रा | kutrā | कुत्रा भुंकतो. (The dog barks.) |
| Cat | मांजर | māñjar | मांजर दूध पिते. (The cat drinks milk.) |
| Cow | गाय | gāy | गाय गवत खाते. (The cow eats grass.) |
| Horse | घोडा | ghoḍā | घोडा वेगाने धावतो. (The horse runs fast.) |
| Buffalo | म्हैस | mhais | म्हैस पाण्यात बसते. (The buffalo sits in water.) |

## Usage Notes

- **कुत्रा (kutrā)** is masculine, while **मांजर (māñjar)** is neuter
- Animal names have specific genders in Marathi
- **गाय (gāy)** - cow is feminine, while **बैल (bail)** - bull/ox is masculine
