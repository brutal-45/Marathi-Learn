# Sports - खेळ

Sports vocabulary including games, players, and equipment in Marathi.

## General Sports Terms

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Sport | खेळ | kheḷa | खेळ खेळा. (Play sports.) |
| Game | खेळ | kheḷa | खेळ मजेदार होता. (The game was fun.) |
| Match | सामना | sāmanā | सामना आज आहे. (The match is today.) |
| Tournament | स्पर्धा | spardhā | स्पर्धा जिंकली. (Won the tournament.) |
| Team | संघ | saṃgha | संघ चांगला खेळला. (The team played well.) |
| Player | खेळाडू | kheḷāḍū | तो चांगला खेळाडू आहे. (He is a good player.) |

## Usage Notes

- **खेळ (kheḷa)** means both "sport" and "to play"
- Cricket is extremely popular in Maharashtra
- **कबड्डी (kabaḍḍī)** is a traditional Indian team sport
- Traditional games like **गिल्ली-डंडा (gillī-ḍaṇḍā)** are still played in villages
- **कुस्ती (kustī)** has a rich history in Maharashtra
