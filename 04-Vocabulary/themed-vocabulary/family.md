# Family - कुटुंब

Family members and relationships in Marathi.

## Immediate Family

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Mother | आई | āī | माझी आई खूप प्रेमळ आहे. (My mother is very loving.) |
| Father | बाबा | bābā | बाबा ऑफिसला गेले आहेत. (Father has gone to office.) |
| Brother | भाऊ | bhāū | माझा भाऊ शाळेत शिकतो. (My brother studies in school.) |
| Sister | बहीण | bahīṇ | बहीण गाणे गाते. (Sister sings songs.) |

## Extended Family

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Grandmother | आजी | ājī | आजीला कथा सांगायला आवडते. (Grandmother likes to tell stories.) |
| Grandfather | आजोबा | ājobā | आजोबा बागेत काम करतात. (Grandfather works in the garden.) |

## Additional Family Terms

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Family | कुटुंब | kuṭumba | माझे कुटुंब मोठे आहे. (My family is big.) |
| Son | मुलगा | mulgā | तो एक चांगला मुलगा आहे. (He is a good son.) |
| Daughter | मुलगी | mulgī | मुलगी वाचन करते. (Daughter is reading.) |
| Uncle (father's brother) | काका | kākā | काका गावी राहतात. (Uncle lives in the village.) |
| Aunt (father's brother's wife) | काकू | kākū | काकू जेवण बनवतात. (Aunt is cooking.) |
| Uncle (mother's brother) | मामा | māmā | मामा येत आहेत. (Uncle is coming.) |
| Aunt (mother's brother's wife) | मावशी | māvśī | मावशी गाणे ऐकतात. (Aunt listens to songs.) |

## Usage Notes

- **आई (āī)** and **बाबा (bābā)** are the most common terms for parents
- **भाऊ (bhāū)** refers to brother, while **बहीण (bahīṇ)** refers to sister
- Marathi has specific terms for relatives based on whether they are from father's or mother's side
