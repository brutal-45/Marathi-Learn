# Festivals - सण आणि उत्सव

Festival vocabulary including celebrations, rituals, and traditions in Marathi.

## General Festival Terms

| English | Marathi | Transliteration | Example Sentence |
|---------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Festival | सण | saṇa | सण आनंदाचा असतो. (Festival is joyful.) |
| Celebration | उत्सव | utsava | उत्सव साजरा करा. (Celebrate the festival.) |
| Holiday | सुट्टी | suṭṭī | सुट्टी आहे. (It's a holiday.) |
| Celebration | साजरा करणे | sājarā karaṇe | दिवाळी साजरी करू. (Let's celebrate Diwali.) |
| Joy | आनंद | ānanda | आनंद वाटतो. (Feeling joy.) |
| Tradition | परंपरा | paraṃparā | परंपरा पाळा. (Follow the tradition.) |

## Usage Notes

- **सण (saṇa)** refers to a festival or holiday
- **उत्सव (utsava)** means celebration or festival
- **गणेश चतुर्थी (gaṇeśa caturthī)** is the most celebrated festival in Maharashtra
- **गुढी पडवा (guḍhī paḍavā)** marks the Marathi New Year
- **पुरण पोळी (puraṇa pōḷī)** is a traditional sweet flatbread made during festivals
