# Directions - दिशा

| West | पश्चिम | paścima | पश्चिमेकडे सूर्य मावळतो. (Sun sets in the west.) |

## Usage Notes

- **उत्तर (uttara)** means both "north" and "answer"
- **वर (vara)** and **खाली (khālī)** are used for vertical positions
- **पुढे (puḍhe)** and **मागे (māge)** indicate forward and backward
- **डावे (ḍāve)** and **उजवे (ujave)** change based on gender: डावीकडे (left side)
- Directions often use **-कडे (-kaḍe)** suffix: उत्तरेकडे (towards north)
