## The Indian Numbering System | भारतीय संख्या प्रणाली

The Indian numbering system differs from the Western system. Instead of millions and billions, we use:

भारतीय संख्या प्रणाली पाश्चात्य प्रणालीपेक्षा वेगळी आहे. दशलक्ष आणि अब्ज ऐवजी, आपण वापरतो:

| Indian Term | Marathi | Value | Western Equivalent |
|-------------|---------|-------|-------------------|
| Hundred | शंभर (śambhar) | 100 | Hundred |
| Thousand | हजार (hajār) | 1,000 | Thousand |
| Lakh | लाख (lākh) | 100,000 | Hundred Thousand |
| Crore | कोटी (koṭī) | 10,000,000 | Ten Million |
| Hundred Crore | शंभर कोटी | 1,000,000,000 | Billion |

## Tips for Learning Numbers | संख्या शिकण्यासाठी टिप्स

1. **Practice writing Devanagari numerals** - Write them alongside Arabic numerals
2. **Count everyday objects** - Practice counting items around you
3. **Use numbers in context** - Learn them within sentences, not in isolation
4. **Practice pronunciation** - Listen and repeat the sounds
5. **Start small** - Master 0-20 before moving to larger numbers

---

## Cultural Notes | सांस्कृतिक टीपा

- Numbers are written from left to right, same as English
- In formal writing, Devanagari numerals (०, १, २...) are often used
- In everyday life, Arabic numerals (0, 1, 2...) are commonly used
- The lakh and crore system is used in official documents, news, and daily conversation
- When saying phone numbers, each digit is pronounced individually
