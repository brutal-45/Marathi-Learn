# Number Writing Practice | संख्या लेखन सराव

## Introduction | परिचय

This exercise focuses on writing numbers in Devanagari script. Practice is essential for mastering the Marathi number system.

## Practice Tips | सराव टिप्स

1. **Write daily** - Practice writing numbers every day
2. **Use in context** - Write numbers when noting dates, prices, etc.
3. **Compare scripts** - Write Arabic and Devanagari side by side
4. **Say it out loud** - Pronounce numbers while writing
5. **Start small** - Master 0-10 before moving to larger numbers
