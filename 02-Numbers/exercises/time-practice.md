# Time Practice Exercises | वेळ सराव अभ्यास

## Introduction | परिचय

This exercise set focuses on telling time, understanding time expressions, and using time-related vocabulary in Marathi.

या सरावात वेळ सांगणे, वेळेची व्यक्तीकरणे समजणे आणि वेळ-संबंधित शब्दसंग्रह वापरणे यावर लक्ष केंद्रित केले आहे.

### Time Format | वेळ स्वरूप

| Pattern | Marathi | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| X वाजले आहेत | X vājale āhet | It is X o'clock |
| X वाजून Y मिनिटे झाली | X vājūn Y miniṭe jhālī | X hours Y minutes passed |
| सव्वा X | savvā X | Quarter past X |
| साडे X | sāḍe X | X and a half |
| पावणे Y | pāvṇe Y | Quarter to Y |

## Practice Tips | सराव टिप्स

1. **Look at clocks** - Throughout the day, say the time in Marathi
2. **Ask and answer** - Practice asking "What time is it?" and answering
3. **Daily routine** - Write your daily schedule in Marathi
4. **Set alarms** - Set phone alarms and say the time in Marathi when they ring
5. **TV guide** - Look at TV schedules and read times in Marathi
