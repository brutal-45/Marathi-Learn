# Ordinal Numbers | क्रमवाचक संख्या

## Introduction | परिचय

Ordinal numbers (क्रमवाचक संख्या) indicate position or order in a sequence. They answer the question "Which one?" or "In what order?" Examples include first, second, third, etc. In Marathi, ordinal numbers are formed by adding suffixes to cardinal numbers.

क्रमवाचक संख्या क्रम किंवा स्थान दर्शवतात. या "कोणता?" किंवा "कोणत्या क्रमाने?" या प्रश्नांची उत्तरे देतात. उदाहरणार्थ: पहिला, दुसरा, तिसरा इत्यादी.

## Part 4: Formation Pattern | भाग ४: बनण्याचा पॅटर्न

### Basic Rule | मूळ नियम

Most ordinal numbers are formed by adding the suffix **-वा (-vā)** for masculine, **-वी (-vī)** for feminine, or **-वे (-ve)** for neuter to the cardinal number.

| Cardinal Number | Add Suffix | Ordinal Number |
|-----------------|------------|----------------|
| पाच (five) | -वा | पाचवा (fifth) |
| सहा (six) | -वा | सहावा (sixth) |
| सात (seven) | -वा | सातवा (seventh) |
| दहा (ten) | -वा | दहावा (tenth) |

### Special Cases | विशेष प्रकरणे

Some ordinal numbers have irregular forms:

| Cardinal | Expected | Actual Ordinal | Note |
|----------|----------|----------------|------|
| एक (one) | *एकवा | पहिला (pahilā) | Completely irregular |
| दोन (two) | *दोनवा | दुसरा (dusarā) | Irregular form |
| तीन (three) | *तीनवा | तिसरा (tisarā) | Slight modification |
| चार (four) | *चारवा | चौथा (chauthā) | Modified form |
