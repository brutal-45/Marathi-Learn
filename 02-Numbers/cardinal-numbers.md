# Cardinal Numbers | मूळ संख्या

## Introduction | परिचय

Cardinal numbers (मूळ संख्या) are used for counting and indicating quantity. They answer the question "How many?" This comprehensive guide covers everything from basic counting to the unique Indian numbering system.

मूळ संख्या मोजण्यासाठी आणि प्रमाण दर्शवण्यासाठी वापरल्या जातात. या "किती?" या प्रश्नाचे उत्तर देतात.

---

## Learning Objectives | शिक्षण उद्दिष्ट्ये

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

- Count from 0 to 100 in Marathi
- Form compound numbers (21-99)
- Use the Indian numbering system (lakh, crore)
- Read and write Devanagari numerals
- Apply numbers in everyday contexts

## Part 2: Numbers 11-20 | भाग २: संख्या ११-२०

Numbers 11-19 have unique names in Marathi. Notice the patterns.

| # | English | Marathi Word | Devanagari Numeral | Transliteration | Breakdown |
|---|---------|--------------|-------------------|-----------------|-----------|
| 11 | Eleven | अकरा | ११ | akarā | ak (1) + rā |
| 12 | Twelve | बारा | १२ | bārā | bā + rā |
| 13 | Thirteen | तेरा | १३ | terā | te + rā |
| 14 | Fourteen | चौदा | १४ | chaudā | chau + dā |
| 15 | Fifteen | पंधरा | १५ | pandharā | pandha + rā |
| 16 | Sixteen | सोळा | १६ | soḷā | so + ḷā |
| 17 | Seventeen | सतरा | १७ | satarā | sata + rā |
| 18 | Eighteen | अठरा | १८ | aṭharā | aṭha + rā |
| 19 | Nineteen | एकोणीस | १९ | ekoṇīs | ek + oṇīs |
| 20 | Twenty | वीस | २० | vīs | vīs |

### Pattern Notes | पॅटर्न टीपा

- Numbers 11-18 mostly end with "रा" (rā) sound
- Number 14 changes slightly: चौदा (chaudā) from चार (chār)
- Number 19: एकोणीस (ekoṇīs) literally means "one less than twenty"
- Number 20: वीस (vīs) is unique

### The Indian Numbering System | भारतीय संख्या प्रणाली

India uses a unique numbering system with terms like "lakh" and "crore" instead of million and billion.

| Indian Term | Marathi | Value | Arabic Numerals | Western Equivalent |
|-------------|---------|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Lakh | लाख | 1,00,000 | 100,000 | Hundred Thousand |
| Ten Lakh | दहा लाख | 10,00,000 | 1,000,000 | Million |
| Crore | कोटी | 1,00,00,000 | 10,000,000 | Ten Million |
| Ten Crore | दहा कोटी | 10,00,00,000 | 100,000,000 | Hundred Million |
| Hundred Crore | शंभर कोटी | 1,00,00,00,000 | 1,000,000,000 | Billion |
