# Vocabulary Flashcards

Print this page and cut along the dotted lines to create your Marathi vocabulary flashcards.

---

## How to Use These Flashcards

1. **Print** this document on thick paper or cardstock
2. **Cut** along the dotted lines to separate cards
3. **Fold** each card in half (Marathi on one side, English on the other)
4. **Practice** by looking at one side and guessing the other

## Quick Reference Card (Print & Keep)

```
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           MARATHI QUICK REFERENCE                     │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Pronouns:                                            │
│  मी (mī) = I    आम्ही (āmhī) = We                      │
│  तू (tū) = You (informal)   तुम्ही (tumhī) = You (formal)│
│  तो (to) = He   ती (tī) = She   ते (te) = They        │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Question Words:                                      │
│  काय (kāy) = What     कुठे (kuthe) = Where            │
│  कधी (kadhī) = When   कोण (koṇ) = Who                 │
│  कसे (kase) = How     का (kā) = Why                   │
├───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Useful Verbs:                                        │
│  आहे (āhe) = is/am/are                               │
│  आहेत (āhet) = are (plural)                          │
│  नाही (nāhī) = no/is not                             │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Tips for Using Flashcards

1. **Daily Practice**: Review 10-15 cards every day
2. **Shuffle**: Mix up the order to avoid memorizing sequence
3. **Say Aloud**: Pronounce each word when studying
4. **Group Learning**: Practice with a partner
5. **Track Progress**: Mark cards you know well vs. need to review
