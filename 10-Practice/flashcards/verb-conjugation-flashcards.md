# Verb Conjugation Flashcards

Master Marathi verb conjugations with these comprehensive flashcards. Print and cut to create your practice deck.

---

## How to Use These Flashcards

1. **Print** this document on thick paper
2. **Cut** along the dotted lines
3. **Study** by covering one side and testing yourself
4. **Practice** regularly for best results

---

## Quick Reference: Conjugation Patterns

### Present Tense Endings

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|--------|----------|--------|
| First (मी/आम्ही) | -तो/-ते | -तो |
| Second (तू/तुम्ही) | -तोस/-तेस | -ता |
| Third (तो/ती/ते) | -तो/-ते | -तात |

### Present Continuous (with आहे)

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|--------|----------|--------|
| First | आहे | आहोत |
| Second | आहेस | आहात |
| Third | आहे | आहेत |
