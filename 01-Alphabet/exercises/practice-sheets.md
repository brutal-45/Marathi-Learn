## Tips for Effective Practice | प्रभावी सराव टिप्स

1. **Write daily:** Consistency is more important than duration
2. **Say aloud:** Always pronounce what you write
3. **Use proper stroke order:** Build good habits from the start
4. **Review mistakes:** Learn from errors
5. **Practice with others:** Study with a partner if possible
